Our Signature Techniques

Each technique brings a unique look and feel to your custom piece. Discover what makes our designs stand apart.

What makes our drinkware and custom designs special? It is the techniques we use. Each one is mastered by hand, perfected through hundreds of creations, and applied with care to every single piece. No two items are ever exactly alike.

Technique 01

Snowglobe

Floating particles sealed inside a double-walled glass — shake it and watch the magic. Each snowglobe cup is a miniature wonderland you can drink from. The particles are permanently sealed and the bamboo lid completes the premium look.

"Like holding a snow globe you can drink from."
1Design layout and select particles
2Seal particles inside the double wall
3Cure and quality check
4Finish with bamboo lid

Technique 02

Goopy

Hand-painted drips cascading down the surface in organic, flowing patterns. Each goopy design is completely unique because the drips fall differently every time. Bold, colorful, and impossible to replicate exactly.

1Apply base coat color
2Hand-drip contrasting colors
3Cure under controlled heat
4Seal with protective clear coat
Starting from $35

3Cure for durability
4Finish with protective seal

Technique 05

DTF Printing

Direct-to-Film printing delivers vibrant, full-color designs that last wash after wash. Perfect for custom apparel — shirts, sweaters, hoodies, and more. Works beautifully on any fabric color, from white to black and everything in between.

1Print design onto special film
2Apply adhesive powder
3Heat-press onto garment
4Peel and quality check
Starting from $25
Order with this technique

Technique 06

Sublimation

Sublimation permanently fuses your design into the surface of ceramic, glass, and coated materials. The result is a photo-quality, dishwasher-safe finish that will not peel, crack, or fade. Ideal for mugs, glass cans, and specialty drinkware.

1Print design on sublimation paper
2Wrap and secure onto item
3Heat-press to fuse permanently
4Cool and inspect quality
Starting from $20
Order with this technique

3Print and post-process
4Finish and quality check
