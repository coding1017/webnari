Common Questions

How often should I review my insurance coverage? +
We recommend reviewing your coverage annually or whenever major life changes occur, such as purchasing a home, getting married, or starting a business. Major changes in your situation could require coverage adjustments.
What factors determine my insurance rates? +
Rates depend on multiple factors including your age, location, claims history, coverage limits, deductibles, credit score, and the type of property or vehicle being insured. We work to find the best rates based on your profile.
Do you offer discounts? +
Yes, we offer multiple discounts including bundling (combining home and auto), good driver discounts, claims-free discounts, safety feature discounts, and more. We'll identify all available discounts for your situation.
What happens if I have a claim? +
Simply contact us immediately. We guide you through the entire process, connect you with adjusters, and ensure all documentation is submitted properly. Our team stays involved to help resolve your claim efficiently.
Can I manage my policy online? +
Absolutely. Our client portal provides 24/7 access to view your policies, request changes, access documents, and update your information. You can also contact us by phone or email anytime.
What's your service area? +
We serve the entire South Florida region including Miami-Dade, Broward, and Palm Beach counties. We have local expertise combined with access to national carriers for comprehensive coverage options.
