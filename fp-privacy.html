Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Introduction

FlowPro Plumbing LLC ("FlowPro," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using our website at flowproplumbing.com or scheduling service with us, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Request a service estimate or schedule an appointment
  • Submit a contact form or call our phone line
  • Pay for services rendered
  • Subscribe to our email newsletter

The types of information we may collect include:

  • Name, phone number, and email address
  • Service address and property information
  • Payment information (processed securely via third-party processors)
  • Service history and job notes
  • Device and usage data when you visit our website (IP address, browser type, pages visited)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Schedule, dispatch, and perform plumbing services
  • Process payments and send invoices
  • Send appointment confirmations and service reminders
  • Communicate about your service history or warranties
  • Send occasional marketing emails about promotions and tips (you may opt out at any time)
  • Improve our website and service quality
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
To opt out of marketing communications, email us at privacy@flowproplumbing.com or click "unsubscribe" in any email we send.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in limited circumstances:

  • With payment processors to handle billing securely (e.g., Stripe, Square)
  • With scheduling and dispatch software to coordinate service
  • With our employees and contractors who need it to perform services
  • When required by law, court order, or government authority
  • In connection with a business sale or merger (you will be notified)

All third-party service providers we work with are contractually required to protect your information and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses cookies and similar technologies to understand how visitors use our site. This includes analytics cookies (such as Google Analytics) that help us understand traffic patterns and improve the user experience.

You can disable cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some website functionality. We do not use cookies to serve third-party advertising.

Data Retention

We retain customer records, including service history and contact information, for a minimum of 7 years in compliance with Florida contractor licensing requirements (Florida Statutes Chapter 489). Payment records are retained as required by applicable tax laws.

Website usage data collected via analytics tools is retained according to those tools' default retention policies (typically 26 months).

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@flowproplumbing.com. We will respond within 30 days.
