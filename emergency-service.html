Don't Wait — These Are Emergencies

Call Us If You Notice...

Electrical emergencies can escalate from inconvenience to catastrophe in minutes. If you see or smell any of these, stop using the affected circuits and call us immediately.

🔥

Burning Smell

Burning or melting smell from outlets, switches, or your electrical panel — shut off power and call now.

⚡

Sparks or Scorch Marks

Visible sparks when plugging in devices, or dark scorch marks around outlets or the panel cover.

🔁

Breaker Keeps Tripping

Main breaker or circuit breaker that trips repeatedly and won't stay reset — never bypass a breaker.

💡

Partial Power Loss

Some areas of your home have power but others don't — can indicate a failed leg at the utility or panel.

🔊

Buzzing or Humming

Unexplained buzzing, humming, or crackling sounds from walls, outlets, or the breaker panel.

💧

Flooding Near Panels

Water intrusion near your electrical panel or any outlet — do not touch anything. Call before re-entering.
