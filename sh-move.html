FAQ

Common questions.

What does the deposit-back guarantee cover?
If your landlord or property manager withholds any portion of your security deposit due to cleanliness after our move-out service, we return and re-clean the flagged areas at no additional cost. We stand behind our work until standards are met.
How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 48 hours in advance, especially during the first and last weeks of the month when turnover demand is highest. Same-day or next-day service may be available depending on availability.
Do you clean inside appliances?
Yes. Our move-in and move-out service includes the interior of the oven, refrigerator, dishwasher, microwave, and washer-dryer units. We clean shelves, drawers, seals, and drip pans.
Can you coordinate with my property manager?
Absolutely. We routinely coordinate access, scheduling, and walkthrough inspections directly with property managers and landlords. We can also send the photo documentation report to them on your behalf.
