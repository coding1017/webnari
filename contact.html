Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

How quickly can you come out?

+

For non-emergency work we typically schedule within 2–3 business days. We offer same-day availability for many service areas. Emergency calls are responded to within 60 minutes, 24 hours a day, 7 days a week — including holidays.

Do you provide written quotes?

+

Yes, always. We provide flat-rate written quotes before any work begins. No verbal estimates. You'll know the exact total price — parts, labor, permits, and everything — before a single wire is touched. If the scope changes, we discuss it with you first.

Do you pull permits?

+

Absolutely. Every job that legally requires a permit gets one, and we handle all the paperwork with the county. We schedule and attend the final inspection, and we never hand a project off until it has passed. Work done without permits can void your homeowner's insurance and cause problems when you sell.

What areas do you serve?

+

We serve all of Palm Beach County and northern Broward County, including Boca Raton, Delray Beach, Boynton Beach, West Palm Beach, Palm Beach Gardens, Wellington, Jupiter, Lake Worth, Deerfield Beach, Pompano Beach, Fort Lauderdale, and Coral Springs. Call us if you're unsure — we likely cover your area.
