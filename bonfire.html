The Pit

Where Magic Happens

Bonfire Smokehouse is where tradition meets craft. Our pitmaster, Marco Reyes, has perfected the art of low and slow smoking over two decades. Every brisket, every rack of ribs tells a story of patience, precision, and passion.

We source only the finest cuts of meat and hardwoods—oak and cherry—to create the most authentic, deeply flavorful barbecue in Wynwood. No shortcuts. No compromises. Just pure smokehouse tradition.

Frequently Asked

Do you offer catering? +

Absolutely! We cater events throughout South Florida. From corporate lunches to weddings, we'll bring authentic smokehouse BBQ to your celebration. Contact us for custom quotes and menu options.

Are there vegetarian options? +

We offer grilled vegetables, mac & cheese, collard greens, baked beans, coleslaw, and cornbread. While we specialize in meat, our veggie sides are just as lovingly prepared.

Can I make a reservation? +

Yes! Reservations are available for groups of 6 or more. Call us at (305) 555-2850 or use our online form. Walk-ins are always welcome, especially Tue-Thu.

Is BYOB allowed? +

We have a full bar with craft cocktails, local beers, and premium bourbons. BYOB is not permitted, but our selection pairs perfectly with our smoked meats.

Is there parking available? +

Yes! Street parking is available along NW 25th Street and we have a small lot behind the building. Wynwood has plenty of street parking within a short walk.

What about large parties? +

We love hosting large groups! For parties of 15+, we recommend booking in advance. We offer family-style platters, buffet service, and full catering. Call us to discuss your event.
