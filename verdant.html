Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for my closed terrarium? +
Closed terrariums need minimal care. Water lightly once a month (they recycle their own moisture). Place in indirect light. Keep away from direct heat sources. If condensation is heavy, open briefly to release excess moisture.
Can I customize a terrarium to match my décor? +
Absolutely! We offer fully custom commissions. Discuss your style, color palette, size, and plant preferences. Elena will work with you to design a terrarium that complements your space perfectly. Custom orders typically take 1–2 weeks.
Do you ship terrariums outside of Miami? +
Yes! We ship securely throughout the US. All orders are carefully packed with protective materials. Shipping costs vary by location. Contact us for a quote on your custom terrarium.
What's included in the workshop? +
All materials (glass container, soil, plants, stones, tools), expert instruction from Elena, a take-home terrarium you've crafted, and a printed care guide. Refreshments provided. No experience necessary!
What if my terrarium isn't thriving? +
We stand behind our work with a 30-day guarantee. If your terrarium shows signs of decline despite proper care, we'll replace it or offer a full refund. Contact us with photos and care details for troubleshooting first.
How do I book a workshop or custom order? +
Use the contact form below, email us, or call. We'll confirm availability and discuss your specific needs. Deposits are required for custom orders. We typically respond within 24 hours.
