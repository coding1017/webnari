Frequently Asked Questions

Is it scary? 🔑
Most rooms are thrilling but not terrifying. The Séance room has horror elements but is designed for fun rather than fear. We can adjust the intensity for your comfort.
What if we can't escape? 🔑
No worries! Many teams don't escape on their first try. We'll debrief you, show you what you missed, and you can try again or challenge a different room.
Can kids play? 🔑
Yes! We have family-friendly rooms suitable for ages 8+. Some rooms are better for teens and adults. Contact us to find the perfect fit for your group.
How many people per room? 🔑
Most rooms accommodate 2–6 players, with some supporting up to 8. Larger groups can book multiple rooms and compete simultaneously.
Can we play solo? 🔑
We recommend at least 2 players for the best experience and puzzle solving. Solo play is available but may be more challenging.
Is there a dress code? 🔑
Dress comfortably! You'll be moving around, crouching, and exploring. Avoid heels and overly loose clothing for safety.
Can we take photos inside? 🔑
Yes! We encourage you to capture memories. Just no flash or distracting bright lights during the game, please.
What's the cancellation policy? 🔑
Cancellations made 48 hours in advance get a full refund. Less than 48 hours is non-refundable. Reschedules are always welcome!
