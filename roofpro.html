FAQ

Common Questions.

How long does a roof replacement take?
Most residential roof replacements take 1-3 days depending on size and complexity. Larger homes or tile roofs may take up to 5 days. We work efficiently to minimize disruption to your daily routine.
What roofing materials do you recommend for South Florida?
For South Florida, we recommend impact-rated architectural shingles, concrete or clay tile, and standing-seam metal roofing. All offer excellent wind resistance for hurricane season. We help you choose based on budget, aesthetics, and longevity.
Does homeowner's insurance cover roof replacement?
Most homeowner's insurance policies cover roof damage caused by storms, fallen trees, and other covered perils. We work directly with insurance companies and can help you navigate the claims process from start to finish.
How do I know if I need a new roof?
Common signs include missing or curling shingles, leaks or water stains on ceilings, granules in gutters, sagging roof deck, and a roof over 20 years old. We offer free inspections to assess your roof's condition.
Do you offer financing for roof replacement?
Yes. We offer flexible financing options with approved credit, including 0% interest plans for qualifying customers. We also accept insurance assignments and can work with your budget to find the right solution.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. RoofPro is a licensed roofing contractor (License #CCC1560088), fully insured with $2M in liability coverage, and all crew members are background-checked and OSHA certified.
