"Wine is the only artwork you can drink."

Mosto Vino was born from a simple frustration: in a city as cosmopolitan as Miami, it was surprisingly difficult to find a truly honest wine bar — one where the list was driven by curiosity rather than margin, where the staff knew the names of the farmers, and where a glass of something obscure and beautiful was always within reach.

We opened on Miracle Mile in 2019 with 80 labels and a six-seat bar. Today we carry over 350 wines from 18 countries, host six tasting events monthly, and run a wine club with over 200 members who receive exclusive allocations from producers who rarely export at all.

This is not a wine list designed to impress. It's a cellar designed to surprise.

How We Curate

From Vineyard
to Your Glass

01

Producer Relationships

We visit producers in the field — in Burgundy, Piedmont, Ribera del Duero, and beyond. Every label on our list has been tasted in its natural environment, often directly with the winemaker.

02

Cellar Selection

Our selection committee tastes blind against the broader market. We prioritize genuine terroir expression, minimal intervention, and wines that improve meaningfully with time.

03

Storage Excellence

All bottles are stored on-site in our humidity-controlled cellar at 55°F. We track provenance, rotation, and optimal drinking windows for every label we carry.

04

The Experience

You arrive. You sit. A sommelier guides you — whether you want a $14 glass of something natural and fun, or a vertical flight of Barolo. No pretension required.

"Wine should be an adventure, not an examination."

Whether you're a seasoned collector or picking up your first bottle of natural wine, our team meets you exactly where you are — with warmth, honesty, and genuine excitement about what's in the glass.
