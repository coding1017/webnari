Meet The Chef

HI, I'M JADE

I grew up watching my family cook incredible Chinese food from scratch — recipes passed down through generations. When I started my fitness journey, I realized that most meal prep services were bland, boring, and full of shortcuts.

So I started cooking for friends. Then friends of friends. Then strangers who became friends. Every meal I make comes from the same place: a genuine love for food and a deep care for what you put in your body.

I'm not a corporation. I'm the person who shops for your organic broccoli at 6am, hand-folds your dumplings, and tracks your macros because I want you to hit your goals as badly as you do.
