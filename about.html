Our Story

From One Truck
to South Florida's
Most Trusted Team

VoltPro Electric was founded by master electrician David Reyes in 2009, after 12 years working with a national electrical contractor. After watching customers get upsold on work they didn't need and watching crews cut corners that left homes less safe than when they started, David had enough.

He started VoltPro with one truck, a single apprentice, and a commitment that every job would be done right — flat-rate pricing, every permit pulled, every installation inspected. No exceptions.

Sixteen years later, VoltPro Electric employs 18 licensed electricians, has completed over 3,200 projects across Palm Beach and Broward County, and holds Florida electrical contractor license EC13009274. The commitment hasn't changed. Neither has the work ethic.

We're not the cheapest electrician in South Florida. We're the one you won't have to call back.
