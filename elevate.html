The Venue

Portland's Most
Elevated Experience

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Portland's Old Town Chinatown, Elevate Nightclub has quickly become the city's most sought-after nightlife destination. Our venue combines world-class sound engineering with kinetic lighting design and an intimate, crowd-forward layout that puts you at the center of the energy.

From high-octane hip-hop takeovers and Latin reggaeton nights to exclusive live performances by nationally touring artists, every night at Elevate is crafted to be extraordinary. Whether you're celebrating at a VIP table or losing yourself on the dance floor, this is where Portland comes alive.
