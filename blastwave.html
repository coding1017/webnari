FREQUENTLY ASKED

Is pressure washing safe for all surfaces? ▼
Great question! We use different pressure levels and soft wash techniques for delicate surfaces like siding, roof, and wood. High pressure is reserved for concrete and asphalt. Your surfaces are always protected.
How often should I get my property washed? ▼
For residential properties, we recommend annual pressure washing to maintain appearance and extend surface life. High-traffic areas and commercial properties may need cleaning 2-3 times yearly. We'll advise you based on your specific property.
What's the difference between pressure and soft washing? ▼
Pressure washing uses high-pressure water jets (2,500+ PSI) for tough surfaces like driveways. Soft washing uses lower pressure (500-1,500 PSI) with eco-friendly cleaning solutions for delicate surfaces. We choose the right method for each job.
Do you offer emergency services? ▼
Yes! We offer 24/7 emergency pressure washing for urgent situations. Graffiti removal, flood cleanup, and event preparation are available on short notice. Call or text for emergency service availability.
Are your cleaning solutions eco-friendly? ▼
We prioritize eco-friendly solutions for most jobs. Our cleaning agents are biodegradable and safe for your landscape, pets, and the environment. We'll use the most appropriate solution for your specific cleaning needs.
How far do you service from Doral? ▼
We service the entire Miami-Dade County area including Miami, Coral Gables, Homestead, Miami Beach, and more. We're happy to discuss areas outside our primary service zone. Contact us for your specific location.
