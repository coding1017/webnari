Industrial &
Warehouse Cleaning

Heavy-duty cleaning solutions for warehouses, factories, distribution centers, and large-scale facilities. OSHA-compliant protocols that keep your operation running safely.

Capabilities

Built for heavy duty.

Floor Scrubbing
Industrial auto-scrubbers and ride-on machines for concrete, epoxy, and sealed warehouse floors. Deep extraction that lifts embedded grime.
Loading Dock Cleanup
Debris removal, oil stain treatment, and power scrubbing for loading bays, dock levelers, and staging areas that take daily abuse.
Pressure Washing
Hot and cold water pressure washing up to 4,000 PSI for exterior walls, concrete pads, truck bays, and drainage channels.
Grease Removal
Specialized degreasers and steam cleaning for food processing zones, mechanical areas, and any surface with heavy grease buildup.
Equipment Cleaning
Safe, methodical cleaning of conveyor systems, racking hardware, forklifts, and stationary machinery without disrupting operations.
High Ceiling Care
Aerial lift and extension pole work for overhead beams, ductwork, lighting fixtures, sprinkler heads, and rafters up to 60 feet.
Pallet Rack Cleaning
Systematic rack-by-rack dusting, degreasing, and debris removal. We work around live inventory to minimize downtime.
OSHA Compliance
Documented cleaning protocols, SDS-compliant chemical handling, lockout/tagout awareness, and full PPE on every job. Audit-ready records.

FAQ

Common questions.

How large of a facility can you handle?
We regularly service warehouses and distribution centers from 10,000 to over 500,000 square feet. Our fleet includes ride-on scrubbers, aerial lifts, and multi-crew capacity to handle any scale efficiently.
Can you work overnight or on weekends?
Absolutely. Most of our industrial clients prefer off-hours cleaning to avoid disrupting operations. We offer flexible scheduling including overnight, weekends, and holiday shifts at no extra charge.
Are your chemicals safe around food and inventory?
Yes. We use EPA-registered, food-safe degreasers and sanitizers where required. All chemical Safety Data Sheets are provided in advance, and our team follows strict containment and rinse protocols.
Do you provide OSHA documentation?
Every job includes a post-clean report with photos, chemical SDS records, and compliance documentation. We keep digital records on file for your audits and inspections.
