FAQ

Common Questions.

How fast can a locksmith get to me?
Our average response time is 30 minutes or less anywhere in the Miami-Dade area. We have mobile units stationed across South Florida for rapid dispatch 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
How much does a lockout service cost?
Residential lockout services start at $65. We provide upfront pricing before any work begins. There are no hidden fees, no hourly surprises, and no charges for the estimate itself.
Can you make a car key without the original?
Yes. We can cut and program transponder keys, key fobs, and smart keys for most vehicle makes and models, even without the original key. Our mobile units carry the equipment needed to do this on-site at your location.
What is lock rekeying vs. lock replacement?
Rekeying changes the internal pins so old keys no longer work, without replacing the lock hardware. It costs less than full replacement and is ideal after moving into a new home, losing a key, or ending a lease.
Do you install smart locks?
Yes. We install and configure smart locks from Schlage, Yale, August, and other leading brands. We ensure proper integration with your existing deadbolt and door hardware, and walk you through the setup.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. LockPro is a state-licensed, bonded, and fully insured locksmith service. Our license number is LK2024-00451. We carry $2M in general liability coverage for your protection.
