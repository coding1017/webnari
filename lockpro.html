Our Services

COMPLETE LOCKSMITH SOLUTIONS

From emergency lockouts to high-security commercial installations, we have the expertise and tools for every lock and key situation.

Emergency Lockout

Locked out of your home, office, or car? Our technicians arrive in under 30 minutes with the tools to get you back inside without damage.

Lock Rekeying

New home, lost keys, or security concern? We rekey your existing locks so old keys no longer work — faster and more affordable than full replacement.

Smart Lock Install

Upgrade to keyless convenience with professional smart lock installation. We work with all major brands including August, Yale, Schlage, and Kwikset.

Commercial Security

Master key systems, access control, high-security locks, and panic hardware for offices, retail, and commercial properties of all sizes.

Car Key Replacement

Transponder keys, key fobs, and smart keys for all vehicle makes and models. Programmed on-site so you can drive away immediately.

Safe Opening

Lost your safe combination? We open residential and commercial safes without damage, and can repair or replace locks and combinations on-site.
