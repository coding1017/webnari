Warning Signs

Signs You Have a Clogged Drain

South Florida's hard water and aging sewer infrastructure means clogs form fast. Catching the signs early saves you from costly water damage and sewage backups.

Slow Drains

Water pooling in the sink, shower, or tub that takes minutes to drain is the earliest sign of a developing clog. Grease, hair, and mineral buildup gradually narrow the pipe until flow stops completely.

Gurgling Sounds

A gurgling or bubbling sound coming from your drain after water runs means trapped air is being forced through a partial blockage. This sound is especially common in main sewer line clogs affecting multiple fixtures at once.

Bad Odors

Foul sewer smells rising from drains indicate organic material — food, grease, or waste — trapped and decomposing in your pipes. Persistent odors that don't clear with flushing signal a deeper blockage needing professional attention.

Recurring Clogs

If the same drain clogs repeatedly within weeks of being cleared, a store-bought snake isn't reaching the root cause — tree root intrusion, pipe collapse, or heavy grease buildup deep in the line demands professional hydro-jetting or camera inspection.

What We Clear

Our Drain Cleaning Services

From a single clogged bathroom sink to a blocked main sewer line, FlowPro has the tools and experience to restore flow fast.

Kitchen Drains

Grease, food particles, and soap scum are the top causes of kitchen drain clogs. We clear blockages at the P-trap, branch line, and grease traps using drain snakes and enzymatic treatments that break down buildup at the source.

Bathroom Drains

Hair and soap are the primary culprits in bathroom sink, shower, and tub clogs. Our technicians remove blockages completely rather than just punching through them — ensuring longer-lasting results and no debris pushed further into the line.

Main Sewer Line

A blocked main sewer line affects every drain in the house simultaneously. We use heavy-duty electric drain machines and hydro-jetting equipment capable of clearing the most stubborn obstructions — including tree roots — from 4-inch to 6-inch sewer mains.

Hydro-Jetting

Our hydro-jetting service uses high-pressure water — up to 4,000 PSI — to completely scour pipe walls clean. Unlike snaking, which only punches a hole through a clog, hydro-jetting removes grease, scale, and debris entirely, restoring pipes to near-original diameter.

Camera Inspection

When clogs keep coming back, a drain camera tells you exactly what is happening inside the pipe. We send a high-definition waterproof camera into the line and record the footage so you can see cracks, root intrusion, or collapsed sections before deciding on repairs.

Floor Drains & Cleanouts

Garage floor drains, laundry room drains, and exterior cleanouts collect debris year-round. We clear and flush these lines to prevent backups during heavy South Florida rain events, and ensure your cleanout access points are clear for future service.
