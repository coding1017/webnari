What We Cover

Every detail, handled.

Debris Removal
Lumber scraps, drywall offcuts, packaging materials, and bulk waste cleared from every room, hallway, and stairwell.
Dust Extraction
HEPA-filtered vacuuming of all surfaces including ceilings, walls, ledges, and air vents. No dust left behind.
Window Cleaning
Interior and exterior glass cleaned of stickers, paint residue, caulk smears, and construction film. Crystal-clear results.
Floor Restoration
Tile scrubbing, hardwood buffing, grout cleaning, and carpet extraction. Every floor surface restored to its original finish.
HVAC Vent Cleaning
All supply and return vents vacuumed and wiped. Prevents construction dust from cycling through the air system after move-in.
Paint Overspray
Careful removal of paint drips, overspray, and tape residue from trim, hardware, glass, and countertops without surface damage.
Fixture Detailing
Light fixtures, outlet covers, cabinet hardware, and plumbing fixtures wiped, polished, and fingerprint-free.
Final Detail Polish
A white-glove walkthrough covering baseboards, door frames, switch plates, and closet interiors. Move-in ready perfection.

Our Process

Three phases to spotless.

01
Rough Clean
Bulk debris removal, sweeping, and initial wipe-down of all surfaces. We clear the construction waste so tradespeople can complete final installations without obstruction.
02
Detail Clean
HEPA vacuuming, window scraping, paint overspray removal, fixture polishing, and thorough cleaning of kitchens, bathrooms, and utility areas. Every surface addressed methodically.
03
Final Polish
White-glove walkthrough with your project lead. Touch-up cleaning of baseboards, door hardware, light switches, and closet interiors. The space is delivered move-in ready.
FAQ

Common questions.

How soon after construction ends can you start?
We can mobilize within 24 to 48 hours of your final walkthrough. For large-scale projects, we coordinate directly with your general contractor to schedule the rough clean while finish trades are still wrapping up, minimizing downtime.
What size projects do you handle?
Everything from single-unit condo renovations to multi-story commercial build-outs. We scale our crew and equipment to the scope of the project. No job is too small or too large for our team.
Do you provide COIs to our general contractor?
Yes. We carry $2M in general liability and full workers' compensation coverage. Certificates of Insurance are provided within one business day of request, naming any additional insured parties your builder requires.
How is post-construction cleanup priced?
Pricing is based on square footage, number of phases required, and the condition of the site. We provide a detailed written estimate after an on-site walkthrough. No hidden fees and no surprises on the final invoice.

Get a custom quote for your project. We coordinate directly with your builder to keep the timeline on track.
