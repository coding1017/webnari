Your Visit

What to
Expect

First-time patients are often surprised by how relaxing the experience is. Here's what the journey looks like, from booking to breakthrough.

1

Book & Intake

Complete our comprehensive health history intake form before your first visit. We read your pulse, examine your tongue, and listen carefully to your health story — usually 30 minutes for new patients.

2

Diagnosis & Plan

Dr. Patel creates your TCM diagnosis and develops a treatment strategy — which points to needle, which adjunct therapies to include, and how many sessions are recommended for your condition.

3

Treatment

You'll rest comfortably for 30–45 minutes with needles in place, often experiencing warmth, tingling, or profound relaxation. Many patients fall asleep. Moxibustion or cupping may be integrated as needed.

4

Care Plan & Follow-Up

After your session we discuss your response, refine your herbal formula if applicable, and schedule your follow-up. Most patients notice significant change within 4–8 sessions.
