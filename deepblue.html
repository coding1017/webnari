Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Do I need to know how to swim?
Basic swimming ability is required for scuba diving. For snorkeling, we provide flotation vests so you can enjoy the experience even if you're not a strong swimmer. Our Discover Scuba program includes pool training first.
What's included in the price?
All trips include full equipment (mask, snorkel, fins, wetsuit, BCD, regulator, tank), boat transportation, professional guide/instructor, water and snacks. Certification courses also include PADI materials and certification fees.
How deep do you dive?
Snorkeling stays at the surface. Discover Scuba dives reach 30-40 feet. Open Water certified divers go to 60 feet. Advanced divers can explore sites up to 100+ feet including deeper wreck dives.
What marine life will I see?
South Florida reefs are home to sea turtles, nurse sharks, moray eels, eagle rays, barracuda, lobsters, colorful tropical fish, and seasonal visitors like manatees and whale sharks. Every dive is different.
What's your cancellation policy?
Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours in advance. 50% refund within 24-48 hours. No refund within 24 hours. Weather cancellations by us are always fully refunded or rescheduled at no cost.
Do you offer group discounts?
Yes. Groups of 6+ receive 10% off. Corporate team-building and school groups get custom pricing. Private charters are the best value for groups of 8-12 people.
