Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers.

How quickly can you pick up my package?

>

Our average pickup time is 15 minutes from request. Rush deliveries (2-hour) are prioritized and picked up within 30 minutes. We operate Monday-Sunday, 6AM-11PM, with 24/7 emergency dispatch available.

What areas do you service?

>

We cover all of Miami-Dade County and surrounding areas within a 50-mile radius of downtown Miami. This includes Coral Gables, Doral, Hialeah, Kendall, Miami Beach, Brickell, Westchester, South Miami, Homestead, Fort Lauderdale, and Hollywood.

Are deliveries insured?

>

Yes! Every delivery is fully insured up to $10,000. We also offer optional additional coverage for high-value items. Simply declare the value of your package at pickup.

How do I track my delivery?

>

Once your package is picked up, you'll receive a tracking link via text or email. Track in real-time on your phone with GPS updates every 30 seconds. You'll also see a live ETA and driver information.

What payment methods do you accept?

>

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), digital wallets (Apple Pay, Google Pay), ACH bank transfers, and invoicing for corporate accounts. Payment can be handled online, by phone, or at delivery.

Can you deliver to residential addresses?

>

Absolutely! We deliver to homes, apartments, businesses, and commercial sites. Special handling is available for gated communities and secured buildings. Just provide clear instructions at checkout.
