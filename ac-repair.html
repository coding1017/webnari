Warning Signs

Signs You Need AC Repair

South Florida's heat moves fast. If your system is showing any of these signs, don't wait — a small problem can become a full system failure overnight.

Strange Noises

Banging, grinding, squealing, or rattling sounds from your unit are never normal. They often indicate a loose component, failing motor, or refrigerant issue that needs immediate attention.

Warm Air Blowing

If your vents are pushing warm or room-temperature air, your compressor may be failing or refrigerant levels may be low. This is the most common AC complaint in South Florida summers.

High Energy Bills

A sudden spike in your electric bill — without a change in usage — is a strong signal your AC is working harder than it should. Dirty coils, low refrigerant, and failing parts all drive energy costs up.

Ice or Frost on Unit

Ice buildup on your indoor coil or refrigerant lines means restricted airflow or low refrigerant. Running a frozen system can permanently damage the compressor — shut it off and call us right away.
