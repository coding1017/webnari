FAQ

Common questions.

Can I change my plan frequency later?
Absolutely. You can upgrade, downgrade, or adjust your cleaning frequency at any time with 30 days notice. Your new rate takes effect on the next billing cycle.
Is there a minimum contract length?
No. All plans are month-to-month. We earn your business every visit, not through a binding contract. Cancel anytime with 30 days written notice.
What happens if I need to skip a visit?
We understand schedules change. You can skip or reschedule a visit with at least 48 hours notice at no charge. Missed visits without notice may be billed at 50%.
Do you bring your own supplies and equipment?
Yes. Every visit includes all Green Seal certified products, commercial-grade equipment, and supplies. There is nothing for you to provide or purchase separately.
