FAQ

Common Questions.

How often should I mow my lawn in South Florida?
In South Florida's tropical climate, most lawns need mowing every 7-10 days during the growing season (March-October) and every 2-3 weeks during winter months.
What does landscaping service typically cost?
Weekly lawn maintenance starts at $35/visit for standard lots. Full landscape design and installation projects range from $2,500 to $15,000+ depending on scope. We provide free estimates.
Do you handle irrigation system installation?
Yes. We design and install complete irrigation systems including drip lines, sprinkler zones, rain sensors, and smart controllers. We also repair and maintain existing systems.
What's the best grass for South Florida?
St. Augustine grass is the most popular choice for South Florida lawns due to its shade tolerance and lush appearance. Zoysia and Bermuda are excellent alternatives for full-sun areas.
Do you offer one-time cleanup services?
Absolutely. We offer one-time yard cleanups, storm debris removal, and seasonal property preparation. No long-term contract required for cleanup services.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. GreenScape is a licensed landscape contractor (License #LC2024-00892), fully insured with $2M in liability coverage, and all crew members are background-checked.
