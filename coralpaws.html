Our Services

Comprehensive Veterinary Care

From routine wellness check-ups to advanced surgical procedures, we provide everything your pet needs under one roof.

Wellness Exams

Comprehensive annual and semi-annual check-ups, vaccinations, and preventive care plans tailored to your pet's life stage.

Surgery

Spay/neuter, soft tissue surgery, orthopedic procedures, and tumor removals performed with state-of-the-art equipment.

Dental Care

Professional cleanings, digital dental X-rays, tooth extractions, and oral health assessments to keep smiles healthy.

Emergency Care

Urgent medical attention when your pet needs it most. Extended hours and rapid-response protocols for critical situations.

Diagnostics

In-house laboratory, digital X-rays, ultrasound imaging, and blood work for fast, accurate diagnoses.

Exotic Pets

Specialized care for reptiles, birds, small mammals, and other exotic companions. Our team has dedicated exotic training.

FAQ

Common Questions

Yes, we accept all major pet insurance providers including Trupanion, Nationwide, Embrace, and ASPCA Pet Health Insurance. We provide itemized invoices for easy claim submission.
We provide care for reptiles (bearded dragons, geckos, turtles), birds (parrots, cockatiels, finches), small mammals (rabbits, ferrets, guinea pigs, hamsters), and more. Call us if you have a question about your specific pet.
Yes! We partner with CareCredit and Scratchpay to offer flexible payment plans for surgeries and major treatments. We also offer wellness plan subscriptions that spread routine care costs over monthly payments.
We're open Monday through Friday 8AM to 7PM, Saturday 9AM to 5PM, and Sunday 10AM to 3PM. Extended emergency hours are available by calling our after-hours line.
We'll provide detailed pre-surgery instructions at your consultation. Generally, pets should fast for 12 hours before surgery (water is fine). We'll call you the day before to confirm details and answer questions.
We offer medical boarding for pets recovering from surgery or those with special medical needs. For standard boarding and daycare, we can recommend trusted partner facilities in the area.
