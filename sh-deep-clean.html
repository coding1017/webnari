FAQ

Deep clean questions.

How long does a deep cleaning session take?
Duration depends on facility size and condition. A typical 5,000 sq ft office takes 4-6 hours. Larger facilities or heavily soiled spaces may require a full day. We provide a time estimate during the assessment walkthrough.
Are your disinfectants safe for occupied spaces?
Yes. We use EPA-registered, low-VOC products that are safe for people, pets, and the environment. Green Seal certified options are available. Spaces can typically be reoccupied within 30-60 minutes after treatment.
How often should deep cleaning be scheduled?
We recommend quarterly deep cleans for most commercial spaces. High-traffic environments like medical offices, gyms, and food service areas benefit from monthly treatments. We tailor frequency to your facility's needs and traffic patterns.
Do you provide documentation for compliance?
Absolutely. Every deep clean includes a detailed post-service report with photos, products used, dwell times, and areas treated. This documentation supports OSHA, health department, and facility management compliance requirements.
