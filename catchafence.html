FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your fencing project.

Have a question we didn't answer? Contact us and we'll be happy to help.

How do I choose the right fence type for my property?
It depends on your priorities — privacy, security, aesthetics, and budget. During your free consultation, we'll assess your property, discuss your goals, and recommend the best material and style. Wood is great for privacy, aluminum for elegance, vinyl for low maintenance, and chain-link for budget-friendly security.
How long does fence installation take?
Most residential fence installations are completed in 1-3 days, depending on the property size and material. Commercial projects may take longer. We'll provide a specific timeline during your estimate, and we always respect your schedule.
Do I need a permit to install a fence in Orlando?
Yes, most Orlando-area municipalities require permits for fence installation. The good news: we handle all permit applications, HOA submissions, and inspections for you. It's part of our full-service approach — you don't have to deal with any paperwork.
What factors affect the cost of a new fence?
The main factors are: material type (wood, vinyl, aluminum, iron), linear footage, fence height, terrain/grade changes, gate requirements, and any custom design elements. We provide transparent, itemized estimates with no hidden fees.
What warranty do you offer?
We offer a comprehensive workmanship warranty on all installations, plus manufacturer warranties on materials. Vinyl and aluminum fences often come with lifetime material warranties. We'll detail the specific warranty coverage for your chosen materials during the estimate.
Will my fence be HOA compliant?
Absolutely. We're experienced with HOA requirements throughout Central Florida. We'll review your HOA guidelines, submit the required applications on your behalf, and ensure your fence meets all community standards before installation begins.
How do I maintain my new fence?
Maintenance varies by material. Vinyl and aluminum are virtually maintenance-free — just rinse with a hose. Wood fences benefit from staining or sealing every 2-3 years. Iron fences may need occasional touch-up paint. We'll provide specific care instructions for your fence type.
Do I need a property survey before installation?
We recommend knowing your property lines before installation to avoid disputes. If you don't have a recent survey, we can help you arrange one. We also call 811 for utility locates before any digging to ensure a safe installation.
