// Build Process

FROM
IDEA TO RIDE

A structured build process that keeps you informed and in control from first conversation to first pedal stroke.

01

Consultation

Tell us about your riding style, goals, and budget. We listen, ask the right questions, and map out a build or service plan that fits you perfectly.

02

Spec & Quote

We draft a full component spec sheet with options at different price points. Transparent line-item quotes — no surprises, no hidden costs anywhere.

03

Build Day

Our mechanics build your bike using proper workshop tools and torque specs. We document every step and send you photos from the workshop as it comes together.

04

Shake & Handoff

A final road test and quality check before handoff. We walk you through the bike together, dial any remaining adjustments, and you ride away with a smile.
