Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Find answers to questions about our services, process, and more.

What's the difference between temp and permanent placements?
+
Temporary placements are short-term assignments ranging from a few days to several months, perfect for covering absences or managing seasonal workload. Permanent placements are full-time, direct-hire positions where the candidate becomes a full employee of your company. We handle both with equal care and vetting.
How are candidates paid?
+
Zenith handles all payroll for temporary placements. Candidates receive weekly paychecks via direct deposit. For permanent placements, the hiring company manages payroll directly. All tax withholdings and compliance requirements are managed by Zenith for temp assignments.
What industries do you serve?
+
We specialize in Healthcare, Technology, Hospitality, Construction, Administrative, and Logistics sectors across South Florida. Our diverse network of vetted candidates allows us to fill roles across multiple industries with qualified professionals.
What are the minimum requirements for candidates?
+
All candidates must pass a background check, have valid identification, and meet industry-specific certifications if required. We prioritize reliability, work ethic, and professional demeanor. Specific requirements vary by role and client needs.
How quickly can you find candidates?
+
Our average placement time is 48 hours from initial request. Many positions are filled same-day due to our vetted talent pool. Urgent staffing needs are our specialty, and we communicate proactively throughout the process.
Do you offer placement guarantees?
+
Yes. If a temporary placement doesn't work out in the first 3 days, we replace them at no additional cost. For permanent placements, we offer a 90-day guarantee. Our goal is your complete satisfaction.
