How We Make It

From Farm to Scoop

1

Source the Best

We start with the freshest local dairy, seasonal fruits, and premium imports — real Tahitian vanilla, Sicilian pistachios, Valrhona chocolate.

2

Craft the Base

Our pastry chef makes each custard and gelato base from scratch every morning. No mixes, no powders, no shortcuts.

3

Churn Slowly

Small-batch churning at the right speed and temperature creates that impossibly smooth, dense texture you love.

4

Scoop & Enjoy

Served in house-made waffle cones, cups, or pints. Each scoop is generous, each bite is meant to make you close your eyes.

FAQ

Common Questions

Do you have dairy-free options?
Yes. We always have 4-6 vegan flavors made with coconut milk or oat milk. They're labeled clearly in our display case. Our ube coconut and mango sorbet are permanent favorites.
Can I order pints to go?
Absolutely. Pints are $12 and we pack them in insulated bags. We also do quarts ($22) for parties. Call ahead for bulk orders or custom flavor requests.
Do you cater events?
We do. We offer ice cream carts, sundae bars, and pint packages for weddings, corporate events, and private parties. Contact us for a custom quote — we serve all of South Florida.
How often do flavors change?
We rotate about 8-10 flavors per week. Our 6 signature flavors are always available. Seasonal and limited-edition flavors appear on our Instagram before they hit the case.
Is there parking nearby?
Yes. We're in Wynwood with street parking and two paid lots within a block. We're also a short walk from the Wynwood trolley stop.
Do you do custom cakes or ice cream cakes?
Yes, we make ice cream cakes to order. Choose 2 flavors, a waffle crunch layer, and topping. Cakes start at $45 and need 48 hours notice. Perfect for birthdays and celebrations.
