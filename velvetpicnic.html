Each experience is designed, styled, delivered, set up, and packed away entirely by our team. Your only job is to show up, be present, and make memories. We handle everything else.

How It Works

Simple as one, two, three — well, four.

1
Inquire & Consult

Fill out our inquiry form or give us a call. We'll discuss your occasion, vision, date, location, and number of guests to start designing your experience.

2
Customize & Confirm

We send you a detailed proposal and mood board. Choose your color palette, floral style, and add-ons. A 50% deposit secures your date.

3
We Set Everything Up

Our team arrives 90 minutes early to set up your complete experience at your chosen location. Every detail is precisely arranged before you arrive.

4
You Enjoy the Moment

Arrive, be present, make memories. When your experience ends, we return to pack everything up. You leave with nothing but beautiful memories (and great photos).

Common Questions

Everything you need to know

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance, especially for weekends. Peak months (October–May in South Florida) fill quickly. We do occasionally accommodate last-minute requests — reach out and we'll do our best!
South Florida weather can be unpredictable! We monitor forecasts closely and will work with you to reschedule at no charge if rain is likely. If you'd like a covered venue option, we can recommend beautiful indoor garden spaces as alternatives.
Absolutely! While our packages include curated charcuterie boards and beverages, you're welcome to bring additional food and drink. We'll provide a cooler and all serving ware. We can also connect you with catering partners for full meal service.
We work with parks, beach accesses, private residences, rooftops, hotel pool decks, gardens, and more throughout South Florida. Some locations require permits — we handle those on your behalf for a small administrative fee.
Photography is available as an add-on through our partner photographers. A 1-hour session starts at $199. We can also provide setup for self-photography with our ring light and tripod add-on at $45.
Deposits are non-refundable, but fully transferable. If you need to reschedule with 72+ hours notice, we'll apply your deposit to a new date at no additional charge. Cancellations with less than 72 hours notice forfeit the deposit.
