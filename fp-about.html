Our Story

Built on Trust,
Proven by Results

FlowPro Plumbing was founded in 2011 by master plumber Carlos Mendez with one goal: give South Florida homeowners access to honest, fairly priced plumbing work they could actually count on.

Over the past 15 years, we've grown from a one-truck operation to a full team of licensed master plumbers serving 12+ cities across Miami-Dade County. Every technician we hire is background-checked, licensed, and trained to FlowPro's exacting standards.

We don't believe in upselling work you don't need. We give upfront quotes, explain your options clearly, and stand behind every job with a 100% satisfaction guarantee. That's why over 600 of our customers have left us 5-star reviews.
