What We Do

ROADSIDE SERVICES

Stranded, locked out, or in an accident? TowPro has a solution for every roadside emergency. One call and we handle the rest.

Flatbed Towing

Damage-free flatbed transport for all vehicle types. From sedans to luxury exotics, your vehicle rides safely on our modern flatbed fleet with hydraulic lifts and soft straps.

Jump Start

Dead battery? Our technicians arrive with commercial-grade jump packs and battery testers. We'll get you running and test your charging system before we leave.

Lockout Service

Locked your keys in the car? Our trained technicians use professional tools to unlock your vehicle without damage, typically in under 5 minutes.

Tire Change

Flat tire on the highway or in a parking lot? We'll swap it with your spare quickly and safely, or tow you to the nearest tire shop if needed.

Fuel Delivery

Ran out of gas? We deliver enough fuel to get you to the nearest station safely. Regular unleaded, diesel, and premium gasoline available.

Accident Recovery

Post-collision vehicle recovery with winching, debris cleanup, and secure transport. We coordinate with police, insurance, and body shops on your behalf.
