Frequently Asked Questions

We're Here to Help

Do you accept dental insurance?

▼

Yes! We accept most major dental insurance plans. We also offer flexible financing options including payment plans to make dental care accessible to everyone.

What should I expect on my first visit?

▼

Your first visit includes a comprehensive dental exam, X-rays, and a consultation with the doctor. We'll discuss your oral health, answer questions, and create a personalized treatment plan.

Do you offer emergency dental care?

▼

Absolutely! We prioritize emergency cases and often have same-day appointments available. Call us immediately if you're experiencing dental pain or trauma.

How long does professional teeth whitening take?

▼

Our professional whitening treatment takes about one hour and can brighten your smile by up to 8 shades. Results are immediately visible and last for months.

How long do dental implants last?

▼

With proper care and maintenance, dental implants can last a lifetime. They're a permanent solution for missing teeth and function just like natural teeth.

Can adults get Invisalign?

▼

Yes! Invisalign is for anyone, any age. Adults love Invisalign because the clear aligners are virtually invisible and can be removed for eating and cleaning.
