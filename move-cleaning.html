FAQ

Move cleaning questions.

Everything you need to know about our move-in and move-out cleaning service.

How far in advance should I book a move-out cleaning?
We recommend booking at least 3-5 days before your move date to guarantee availability. For peak season (summer months and month-end), 1-2 weeks advance notice is ideal. Emergency same-day cleanings are available when scheduling allows.
Will this cleaning help me get my security deposit back?
Our move-out cleaning is specifically designed to meet or exceed the cleanliness standards required by landlords and property managers. While deposit returns depend on overall property condition, our clients consistently report full deposit recovery on the cleaning portion. We provide before-and-after photos as documentation.
Does the property need to be empty before you clean?
For the best results, the property should be fully emptied of furniture and personal belongings. We can work around remaining items, but an empty unit allows us to reach every surface, corner, and closet for a thorough deep clean.
What's your satisfaction guarantee for move cleanings?
We offer a walkthrough inspection after every move cleaning. If anything does not meet your standards, we re-clean those areas on the spot at no additional charge. If issues are found after we leave, contact us within 24 hours and we return to address them free of charge.
