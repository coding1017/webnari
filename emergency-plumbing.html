WHAT COUNTS AS A PLUMBING EMERGENCY

Plumbing problems escalate quickly. These four situations require immediate professional attention — every minute of delay increases the risk of structural damage, health hazards, and repair costs.

Burst Pipe

A burst pipe can release hundreds of gallons per hour inside walls, ceilings, or under slabs. Turn off your main water supply immediately and call FlowPro. We locate the break, stop the flow, and repair or replace the affected section the same visit.

Sewage Backup

Sewage backing up into toilets, tubs, or floor drains is a health emergency. Raw sewage contains pathogens that pose serious risks. Do not use any fixtures in your home. FlowPro uses camera inspection and hydro-jetting to clear blockages and restore proper drainage fast.

No Hot Water

A sudden loss of hot water can indicate a failed water heater element, gas valve issue, tripped safety cutoff, or — in the case of tank-type heaters — catastrophic failure with leak risk. FlowPro diagnoses and resolves most water heater failures in a single visit.

Flooding and Overflow

Toilet overflow, washing machine line failure, or uncontrolled leaks that are actively flooding a space require immediate shutoff and professional remediation. FlowPro stops the source, assesses the extent of damage, and coordinates with restoration professionals if needed.
