What We Fix

Full-Service Auto Repair

From check engine lights to complete engine rebuilds, we handle all makes and models with the same no-nonsense approach.

Engine Diagnostics

OBD-II scanning, compression testing, and computerized engine analysis. We find the real problem before we touch a wrench.

Brake Service

Pads, rotors, calipers, brake lines, and ABS systems. We inspect the entire brake system and replace only what's needed.

Transmission

Fluid flushes, solenoid repair, clutch replacement, and full rebuilds for automatic and manual transmissions.

A/C & Heating

Refrigerant recharge, compressor replacement, evaporator and condenser repair. Essential for Miami's brutal heat.

Electrical Systems

Alternator, starter, battery, wiring harness diagnostics, and module programming. We trace every short and fault.

Suspension & Steering

Shocks, struts, control arms, tie rods, wheel alignments, and power steering. Restoring that solid, confident ride.
