Our Philosophy

Care that Honors Every Chapter of Life

CareNest was founded in 2013 by Elena Perez, a registered nurse who spent fifteen years in geriatric care before realizing that the most impactful care happens at home — in familiar surroundings, close to loved ones.

We don't place caregivers. We build relationships — between your family, your loved one, and a dedicated professional who genuinely cares.

Every CareNest caregiver is hand-selected, extensively trained, and matched thoughtfully to each client's personality, schedule, and care needs. We believe in consistency — the same caregiver, day after day, building trust and genuine human connection.

All CareNest caregivers hold current Florida HHA (Home Health Aide) or CNA (Certified Nursing Assistant) certification, current CPR/First Aid certification, and have passed Level 2 FBI background screening.
