Got Questions?

All Your Questions, Answered

We want your party planning to be stress-free. Here are the most common questions we get — and if yours isn't here, just reach out!

Ask Us Anything
How much space do I need for a bounce house? +
Our standard bounce castles need a 20×20 ft flat area (grass or concrete both work). We'll ask for your setup location before confirming the booking to make sure everything fits safely.
What if it rains on my party day? +
Light drizzle is usually fine — our inflatables have drainage and are safe in mild weather. In the case of heavy rain, lightning, or winds over 25 mph, we'll work with you to reschedule at no charge. Safety first, always.
Do I need to provide power for the blower? +
The inflatable blower runs on standard 110V power. We need a dedicated outlet within 100 ft of the setup area. If you don't have access, we can rent you a generator for an additional fee.
How far in advance should I book? +
Weekends in spring and summer book up fast — we recommend 2–3 weeks ahead. That said, we often have same-day and next-day availability on weekdays. Call us and we'll do our best!
Is there a deposit required? +
Yes — we require a 25% deposit to hold your date, with the balance due on the day of your event. We accept credit/debit cards, Zelle, and cash. No deposit, no reservation.
Can adults use the inflatables? +
Most of our units have weight limits of 600–800 lbs total occupancy, so yes — adults can definitely join the fun! We just ask that adults be mindful of little ones sharing the space and always follow the posted rules.
