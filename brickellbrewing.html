Our Story

Born in Miami,
Brewed with Pride

Brickell Brewing Co. was founded in 2015 by brothers Marco and Luis Delgado — two Miami natives who grew tired of drinking beer that had nothing to do with Florida. They wanted something local, crafted, and alive.

Starting with a 7-barrel system in a 1,200 sq ft garage in Allapattah, the brothers brewed every weekend, pouring samples at farmers markets and neighborhood cookouts. Within two years, demand forced a move to our current 12,000 sq ft facility in Brickell.

"Great beer is honest. It tells you where it's from, who made it, and why they cared. We put Miami in every batch."

— Marco Delgado, Head Brewer & Co-Founder

Today we brew over 35 different recipes annually, distribute to 200+ South Florida restaurants and bars, and pour 24 rotating taps in our Brickell taproom daily.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked

Everything you need to know before your visit. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach out and we'll get back to you same day.

Contact Us
Do you allow dogs in the taproom? +
Yes! Leashed, well-behaved dogs are welcome on our outdoor patio. We always have a water bowl available, and our staff will fuss over your pup. Inside seating is humans-only.
Is there food at the taproom? +
We partner with local food trucks that park outside Friday–Sunday. We also allow outside food to be brought in. Light snacks (pretzels, nuts, charcuterie) are available at the bar daily.
Do I need to book a tour in advance? +
The Intro Tour runs on weekends at 2PM and 4PM — walk-ins are welcome but booking guarantees your spot. The Deep Dive and Brew Your Own experiences require advance booking and fill up quickly.
Where can I find your beers outside the taproom? +
Our beers are available at 200+ restaurants, bars, and bottle shops across South Florida. Check our website's distribution finder for the closest location. We also ship to select Florida ZIP codes via our online store.
How do I host a private event at Brickell Brewing? +
Our private event room seats up to 80 guests and includes custom beer packages, dedicated staff, and AV equipment. Reach out via our contact form or call us directly. We book 6–8 weeks in advance on weekends.
What's included in the Beer Club membership? +
Members receive 12 cans of exclusive monthly releases (including barrel-aged and small-batch beers not available elsewhere), 15% off all taproom purchases, invitations to members-only events, and first access to new releases. Cancel anytime.
