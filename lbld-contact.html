FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to our most common questions.

How long does a custom order take?
Most custom orders are completed within 5-10 business days depending on complexity and current order volume. Rush orders may be available for an additional fee. We will give you an estimated timeline when you place your order.
What file formats do you accept for designs?
We accept PNG, JPG, SVG, PDF, and AI files. For the best results, we recommend high-resolution PNG or SVG files with transparent backgrounds. If you only have a rough idea or sketch, no worries - we can work with that too.
Can I see a proof before you make it?
Absolutely. We always send a digital proof or mockup for your approval before we start crafting. You will have the chance to request changes before production begins. We want you to be completely happy with the design.
What is the Snowglobe technique?
Our Snowglobe technique involves sealing decorative floating particles inside a double-walled glass. When you shake the cup, the particles swirl around like a snow globe. It is one of our most popular and unique offerings. Learn more about our techniques.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently we ship within the United States. International shipping may be available on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us to discuss international orders and we will do our best to accommodate you.
What is your return policy?
Because each item is custom-made to your specifications, we generally do not accept returns. However, if there is a defect or if the item does not match the approved proof, we will work with you to make it right. Your satisfaction is our priority.
