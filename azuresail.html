FAQ

Before you sail.

Common questions from first-time sailors and seasoned boaters alike. Call us anytime at (305) 555-3000.

Not at all. Every charter includes a fully licensed USCG captain and first mate. You just show up and enjoy — no sailing knowledge required. We also offer sailing lesson add-ons if you want to take the helm yourself.
Sunscreen, a hat, sunglasses, and non-marking soft-soled shoes (or plan to go barefoot on deck). For longer charters, we recommend a light layer for the evening breeze. We provide towels, safety equipment, and all on-board essentials.
Absolutely. For all charters you're welcome to bring your own food and beverages — we have a full galley cooler and ice. For corporate and party charters we also coordinate catering from Miami's top restaurants.
Safety is paramount. If we cancel due to weather (wind over 20 knots, lightning, or NOAA small craft advisories), you receive a full refund or complimentary rescheduling. Client-initiated cancellations within 72 hours are non-refundable.
All charters depart from Miami Beach Marina (Dock 14), just off Alton Road and 17th Street. Easy access with paid parking on-site. We also offer private dock pickup for Brickell and Key Biscayne locations by arrangement.
No minimum. Our sunset sail starts at $350 for a private charter regardless of group size, making it perfect even for couples. Pricing scales with guest count and charter length — contact us for a custom quote.
