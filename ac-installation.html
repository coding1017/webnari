Making the Right Call

Replace or Repair?

We'll always give you an honest recommendation. Here's how to think through the decision before we arrive.

Consider Repair When

Keep the System

  • System is under 8 years old
  • Repair cost is less than 50% of replacement
  • Issue is isolated to a single component
  • System has been well-maintained
  • SEER rating is 14 or higher
Time to Replace When

New System

  • System is 12 or more years old
  • Compressor or coil needs replacement
  • Multiple repairs in the past two years
  • Runs on R-22 refrigerant (phased out)
  • Energy bills have climbed significantly

Our Installation Process

Five steps to a properly installed, fully permitted new system.

1

Free Assessment

We evaluate your home's square footage, insulation, duct condition, and current system to size the right unit accurately.

2

System Selection

We present two or three options at different price points — explaining SEER ratings, warranties, and estimated energy savings for each.

3

Permit Pull

We handle all permit paperwork with the local building department. Every AirPro installation is done to code — no shortcuts.

4

Installation Day

Our certified technicians complete the full installation in one day. We protect your home, clean up completely, and haul away the old system.

5

Final Inspection

County inspection sign-off, system commissioning, and a full walkthrough of your new equipment so you know exactly how it works.
