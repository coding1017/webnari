Our Approach

We believe exceptional interiors emerge from deep listening—understanding not just what our clients want, but who they are. Every project begins with conversation, research, and observation.

Our design process balances aesthetics with pragmatism. We select materials for longevity, arrange spaces for daily living, and infuse personality into every corner. Luxury, for us, is refinement without excess.

  • Functional elegance
  • Curated authenticity
  • Sustainable practices
  • Collaborative process

04 — FAQ
What is your typical project timeline? +

Most residential projects take 12–18 weeks from discovery to completion. Timeline varies based on scope, scope of construction, and material lead times. We provide detailed timelines during the initial consultation.

Do you work within specific budgets? +

Absolutely. We establish clear budgets early and maintain transparency throughout. Design Packages start at $5,000; Full Home projects begin at $25,000. Custom pricing available for all scopes.

What areas do you serve? +

We serve Miami and all of South Florida, including Design District, Coral Gables, Coconut Grove, Miami Beach, Brickell, Key Biscayne, Aventura, Bal Harbour, and Pinecrest.

Can you work with my existing furniture? +

Yes. We assess your existing pieces and incorporate those that align with your new vision. We're skilled at blending old and new to create cohesive, personalized spaces.

How do I start a project with Maison Atelier? +

Contact us via email or phone to schedule a complimentary 30-minute consultation. We'll discuss your vision, budget, and timeline, then propose next steps.

Do you offer virtual consultations? +

Yes. We conduct initial consultations via Zoom or in-person at your property. Once a project begins, we meet regularly to ensure alignment and quality.
