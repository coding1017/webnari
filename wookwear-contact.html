Frequently Asked Questions

How do drops work?
New items drop every Wednesday on Instagram and the shop. Follow @wook.wear to get notified. Items are one-of-a-kind, so once they're sold, they're gone!
Do you take custom orders?
Sometimes! Reach out through the contact form with your idea and I'll let you know if it's something I can make.
What's your shipping policy?
Orders ship within 3-5 business days via USPS. Tracking is provided for every order.
Can I return or exchange?
Due to the handmade, one-of-a-kind nature of each piece, all sales are final. If there's an issue with your order, please reach out and I'll make it right.
Do you ship internationally?
Not yet, but it's on the roadmap! Currently shipping within the US only.
Where can I see your work in person?
Meesh sets up at festivals and markets throughout the year. Follow @wook.wear on Instagram for event announcements.
