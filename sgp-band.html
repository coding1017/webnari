The Musicians

The Collective

Not a fixed band. A handpicked lineup of the finest young acoustic musicians in the game, assembled fresh for every show.

The Sam Grisman Project is built on a simple idea: every show should be its own moment. Rather than a locked-in roster, SGP draws from a deep pool of world-class players across guitar, mandolin, fiddle, banjo, cello, and dobro. Sam handpicks each lineup to fit the venue, the vibe, and the repertoire. The result is something that feels different every night, yet always rooted in the tradition of Dawg music, the Garcia/Grisman sessions, and the endless art of picking among friends.

The Core
Bandleader
Sam Grisman
Bass / Bandleader

Son of mandolin legend David "Dawg" Grisman, Sam grew up in Mill Valley watching Jerry Garcia and his father pick old-time tunes in the living room. Now Nashville-based, he leads the SGP collective, curating lineups and carrying the acoustic tradition forward with a fresh generation of players.
