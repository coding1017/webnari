What We Offer

Precision glass
for every space.

From a single frameless mirror to a complete commercial glazing project, every job receives our full craft and attention.

01
Frameless Shower Enclosures
Custom-measured, fully frameless glass shower doors and enclosures. Ultra-clear low-iron glass, polished hardware in chrome, brushed nickel, matte black, or gold. Sealed and installed for lasting waterproofing.
02
Custom Mirrors
Frameless wall mirrors cut to any shape and size. Beveled, polished, or flat edge finishing. Perfect for bathrooms, gyms, salons, and living spaces. Full installation included.
03
Glass Railings & Balustrades
Tempered safety glass railings for staircases, balconies, pool decks, and mezzanines. Frame-based and standoff (frameless) systems available. Engineering documentation provided for permits.
04
Commercial Storefronts & Glazing
Full commercial glazing services: storefront systems, curtain walls, glass partitions, office dividers, and lobby installations. We work with architectural drawings and coordinate with GCs.
05
Glass Table Tops & Shelves
Tempered or laminated glass panels for dining tables, coffee tables, and custom shelving. Any size and shape, polished or beveled edges. Same-day cutting available for standard sizes.

Glass Repair & Replacement
Cracked or shattered glass in windows, shower doors, mirrors, or table tops. Emergency board-up service available 24/7. Insurance claim documentation provided on request.

For residential shower enclosures, we primarily use 3/8" (10mm) and 1/2" (12mm) fully tempered glass. 3/8" is excellent for standard applications and provides good rigidity. 1/2" is preferred for larger panels, heavy-duty use, or when a premium, substantial feel is desired. We always use safety-rated tempered glass, never standard float glass, in wet areas.
After field measurement, glass fabrication typically takes 5–8 business days. Installation itself usually takes 2–4 hours for a standard enclosure. The caulk and silicone seal requires 24 hours to fully cure before the shower can be used. Full project timeline from initial consultation to use is typically 2–3 weeks.
Yes — we handle all permitting for structural glass installations including railings, balustrades, and commercial glazing. We prepare engineered drawings, submit to the local building department, and coordinate inspections. Our permit coordinator manages the entire process so you don't have to deal with any paperwork or city inspectors.
Standard float glass has a slight green tint due to its iron content — most visible on edge views and in thicker glass. Low-iron glass (also called "ultra-clear" or "Starphire") has up to 90% less iron, resulting in dramatically clearer glass with no green tint. We recommend it for high-end shower enclosures, frameless mirrors, and any application where optical clarity matters most. It costs approximately 20–30% more than standard glass.
