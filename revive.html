TECHNICAL NOTES & ADDENDA

Frequently Asked Questions

NOTE 01: Do you offer emergency service? ▼
Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency appliance repair service. Call (305) 555-2000 anytime for urgent issues. We prioritize emergency calls and dispatch technicians as quickly as possible.
NOTE 02: Is there a service call fee? ▼
No service call fee if we complete the repair the same day. Diagnostic service is always free. If you choose not to proceed with repairs, you'll only pay $75 for the diagnostic.
NOTE 03: What parts do you use? ▼
We use genuine OEM parts for all repairs. This ensures your appliance works as designed and maintains its warranty. We never use cheap knockoff parts.
NOTE 04: Is there a warranty on repairs? ▼
Yes, all repairs come with a 1-year warranty on parts and labor. If the same issue occurs within one year, we'll fix it free of charge.
NOTE 05: Do you work on all brands? ▼
We service all major brands including LG, Samsung, Whirlpool, GE, Maytag, Frigidaire, Bosch, KitchenAid, and more. Call with your brand to confirm.
NOTE 06: How do I schedule a service? ▼
Call us at (305) 555-2000 or fill out the contact form below. We'll discuss your issue and schedule a convenient time. Emergency service available 24/7.
