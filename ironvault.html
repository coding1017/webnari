Our Services

EVERY JOB,
DONE RIGHT.

From new door installation to broken spring repair, IronVault handles residential and commercial door systems across South Florida with a lifetime workmanship warranty.

New Door Installation

Full supply and install of residential and commercial sectional, roll-up, swing, and sliding gate systems. Same-day measurement and next-day install available.

Spring & Cable Repair

Broken spring is the #1 cause of door failure. We carry torsion and extension springs for all door sizes. Most repairs completed within 2 hours of your call.

Opener Installation & Repair

Sales and installation of LiftMaster, Chamberlain, and Genie openers. Smart home integration available. Remotes, keypads, and myQ connectivity.

Hurricane-Rated Doors

Miami-Dade NOA-approved hurricane doors for all impact ratings. Steel, aluminum, and reinforced wood options. Required for all new construction in South Florida.

Security Gate Systems

Sliding, swing, and vertical pivot gate installation for driveways, commercial lots, and apartment complexes. Intercom and access control integration available.

Maintenance Plans

Annual and bi-annual service plans for commercial properties. Includes full system inspection, lubrication, hardware tightening, and balance testing.
