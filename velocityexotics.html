Common Questions

Still Have Questions?

Our concierge team is available 24/7. Don't hesitate to call or text — we love talking cars.

Call (786) 555-2600
What are the age and license requirements? +
You must be at least 25 years old and hold a valid driver's license (US or international). A clean driving record is required. Some high-performance vehicles may require additional documentation.
Is insurance included in the rental price? +
Yes. Basic liability coverage is included. We also offer full comprehensive coverage upgrades for total peace of mind. Our team will walk you through all options at booking.
How far in advance do I need to book? +
We can often accommodate same-day bookings. For peak weekends (holidays, Formula 1 weekends, Art Basel, Ultra Music Festival), we recommend booking 2–4 weeks in advance to secure your preferred vehicle.
Do you offer chauffeur or driven options? +
Yes. We offer chauffeur-driven options for our Rolls-Royce and Bentley fleet, perfect for corporate events, weddings, and airport transfers. Ask us about full-day chauffeur packages.
What is your cancellation policy? +
Cancellations made 72+ hours before pickup receive a full refund. Cancellations within 48 hours forfeit 50% of the rental fee. Same-day cancellations are non-refundable. We understand emergencies — call us.
Where do you deliver within South Florida? +
We deliver to Miami, Miami Beach, Brickell, Coral Gables, Aventura, Fort Lauderdale, Palm Beach, and surrounding areas. Delivery fees apply for locations outside Miami-Dade County.
