Our Philosophy

Rooted in Tradition,
Present in Practice

At Lotus & Stone, we believe yoga is more than physical exercise. It's a practice of presence, a path to understanding the self, and a bridge between body and spirit.

Our name reflects this balance: the lotus represents growth and transformation, while the stone represents groundedness and resilience.

Accessible to All

Every body is a yoga body. We welcome all ages, sizes, and experience levels.

Lineage-Honoring

We teach with respect for yoga's roots and the traditions that shaped it.

Community-Centered

Our studio is a gathering place for seekers, movers, and healers alike.
