Frequently Asked Questions

How long does an express wash take?
+

Our Express Wash typically takes about 15 minutes from start to finish. You can watch as your car gets cleaned with our futuristic equipment and eco-friendly solutions.

Are your cleaning solutions safe for all car types?
+

Yes! We use premium, eco-friendly solutions that are safe for all car types, including luxury vehicles, classic cars, and standard automobiles. Our products protect your paint while removing contaminants.

Can I use my membership at other locations?
+

Currently, memberships are valid at our Doral location. We're expanding to other Miami areas soon. Check back for updates on new AquaBlade locations!

Do you offer mobile car wash services?
+

Not at this time, but our location in Doral offers convenient 24/7 self-service bays and full-service options. Contact us about potential future expansion services.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, mobile payments, and digital wallets. Kiosk and self-service bays also accept cash for convenience.

Is there a cancellation fee for memberships?
+

No cancellation fees! You can pause or cancel your membership anytime with 7 days notice. We want you to feel completely satisfied with your AquaBlade experience.
