Our Story

From Scrap Pile
to Your Table

Brian Dunn started his career building decks and fences as a remodeling carpenter in Portland. He saw how much quality wood was wasted on every job — offcuts, tear-outs, surplus material all headed for the landfill.

Driven by a deep recycling ethic and creative spirit, Brian started diverting that wood and turning it into something beautiful. Today, Closed Loop Woodworks sources leftover material from furniture makers, cabinet shops, mills, and flooring contractors — transforming it into unique, functional art.

Zero Waste Mission
