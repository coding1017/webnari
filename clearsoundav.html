Questions

Before You Reach Out

Most of our clients have similar questions at the start. We've answered the most common ones here — but please don't hesitate to reach out for anything specific to your project.

Schedule a Consultation
How much does a home theater installation cost? +
Costs vary widely based on room size, equipment level, and complexity. A basic dedicated media room starts around $8,000–$15,000. A full premium cinema room with acoustic treatment, 4K laser projection, and Dolby Atmos can range from $25,000–$80,000+. We'll give you a precise quote after our free consultation.
Do you supply the equipment or do I need to source it myself? +
We handle everything — procurement, delivery, installation, and calibration. We have dealer relationships with every major AV brand, which often means better pricing than retail. You can also supply your own equipment if you prefer; we charge a standard integration fee in that case.
How long does a typical installation take? +
A single TV mount or soundbar setup takes 2–4 hours. A full home theater build with wiring rough-in, acoustic panels, and equipment installation typically takes 2–5 days. Whole-home audio and smart home integrations vary by scope — most run 3–7 days across multiple sessions.
Do you work in new construction or just existing homes? +
Both. We partner with architects, interior designers, and general contractors throughout South Florida to plan and rough-in AV systems during construction. Pre-wiring during the build phase saves significant cost and enables more elegant cable management than retrofit installations.
What brands do you work with? +
We're brand-agnostic and work with the full spectrum — Sony, Samsung, LG, Epson, JVC, Klipsch, KEF, Bowers & Wilkins, Sonos, Polk Audio, Denon, Marantz, Control4, Crestron, Lutron, Savant, and many more. We recommend what best fits your room and budget.
Is the consultation really free? +
Yes, completely. We'll visit your home, assess your space and needs, and follow up with a detailed written proposal — all at no charge and with zero obligation. We believe the best projects start with the right design, and you can't design without seeing the space.
