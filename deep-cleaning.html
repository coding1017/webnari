What's included.

Every deep clean covers four critical zones to ensure your facility meets the highest hygiene standards.

Surface Disinfection

EPA-registered hospital-grade disinfectants applied to all surfaces. Desks, counters, door handles, light switches, and shared equipment sanitized to eliminate 99.9% of pathogens.

Floor Deep Clean

Machine scrubbing, extraction cleaning, and protective finish application for all floor types. Includes grout restoration, carpet shampooing, and hard floor stripping and waxing.

Kitchen Sanitization

Commercial degreasing of all surfaces, appliance deep clean inside and out, backsplash scrubbing, and sink sanitization. Food-safe disinfectants for break rooms and prep areas.

Restroom Decontamination

Full fixture disinfection, grout and tile descaling, urinal and toilet deep scrub, mirror and partition sanitization. Odor-neutralizing treatment and supply restocking available.

Questions

Deep cleaning FAQ.

Everything you need to know about our sanitization services.

What makes deep cleaning different from regular cleaning?
Regular cleaning addresses visible dirt and surface maintenance. Deep cleaning goes further with hospital-grade disinfectants, machine scrubbing, extraction cleaning, and targeted treatment of high-risk contamination zones. It reaches behind, under, and inside areas that routine cleaning doesn't touch.
What products do you use for sanitization?
We use EPA-registered, hospital-grade disinfectants that meet CDC guidelines for healthcare facility sanitization. All products carry Green Seal or EPA Safer Choice certification, making them effective against bacteria and viruses while remaining safe for occupants, pets, and the environment.
How long does a deep clean take?
Timing depends on facility size and condition. A standard 5,000 sq ft office typically takes 4-6 hours. Larger facilities or those requiring extensive floor work may need a full day. We can work after hours or on weekends to avoid disrupting your operations.
How often should I schedule a deep clean?
We recommend quarterly deep cleans for most commercial spaces, with monthly sessions for high-traffic environments like medical offices, gyms, and food service areas. Seasonal deep cleans are ideal for offices that maintain regular weekly cleaning schedules.
Do you offer ATP testing to verify results?
Yes. ATP (adenosine triphosphate) bioluminescence testing is available as an add-on service. It provides measurable, objective proof that surfaces meet clinical hygiene standards. Results are documented and provided to you for compliance records.
