Twenty-Seven Years of Mastery

Thread & Bone was founded on the principle that exceptional tailoring is a lost art. In our Design District atelier, we combine traditional hand-craftsmanship with an understanding of modern design.

Every client is unique. Every garment deserves precision. We believe that the perfect fit is not just about measurements—it's about understanding how a garment should move with the body, how it should make you feel.

"A well-tailored garment is an investment in yourself. It's how you present yourself to the world."

Our commitment to excellence has earned us a reputation as Miami's premier atelier for those who demand nothing less than perfection.

Hand-Finished Details

Every stitch is placed with intention. Our artisans employ centuries-old techniques in hand-finishing seams, buttonholes, and hems. The difference is visible in the movement and longevity of each piece.

We source the finest materials and work exclusively with premium fabrics that age beautifully.

Architectural Precision

Bespoke tailoring is architecture for the body. We take precise measurements and create custom patterns that account for individual proportions, posture, and movement patterns.

Pattern-making is both science and art—our master tailors have decades of experience translating your vision into reality.

Sustainable Craftsmanship

We believe in creating garments that last generations. Alterations, restyling, and restoration extend the life of your favorite pieces while reducing waste.

Our approach honors both tradition and responsibility—craftsmanship that respects both the garment and the environment.

Questions

How long does bespoke tailoring typically take? +
Bespoke suits typically require 8-12 weeks from initial consultation to final fitting. This includes two fittings to ensure perfect proportions. Alterations and restyling projects vary based on complexity but generally take 2-4 weeks.
What fabrics do you recommend for bespoke suits? +
We work exclusively with premium Italian and English woolen mills. Your choice depends on climate, intended use, and personal preference. Our team can guide you through options from lightweight tropical wools to structured overcoating materials.
Do you offer alterations on garments not made here? +
Absolutely. We welcome all garments—designer, vintage, or ready-made. Our philosophy is to enhance and perfect any piece. We handle everything from simple hemming to complex reconstruction.
What's included in the initial consultation? +
The initial consultation is complimentary and includes detailed measurements, fabric selection, style discussion, timeline review, and a comprehensive quote. We listen carefully to understand your vision and requirements.
Can you restore vintage or damaged garments? +
Yes. We specialize in restoration work including seam repair, zipper replacement, re-lining, and reinforcement of stress points. Many vintage pieces can be brought back to wearable condition or even improved.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit cards, checks, and bank transfers. For larger projects, we typically require a 50% deposit upon order confirmation with the balance due upon completion.
