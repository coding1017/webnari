Our Craft

ROASTED WITH INTENTION

FROM SEED TO CUP

Every bean we roast tells a story. We travel to origin, meet the farmers, and cup hundreds of samples to find coffees worthy of the Obsidian name.

Our Loring S15 Falcon roaster allows us to craft precise roast profiles that honor each bean's unique character. We roast weekly in small batches to guarantee peak freshness.

Founded in 2018 in a converted warehouse in Wynwood, Obsidian has grown from a one-bag-at-a-time operation to Miami's go-to specialty roaster serving over 40 wholesale partners.

The Process

FOUR STEPS TO PERFECT COFFEE

01

Source

We travel to origin, visit farms, and cup hundreds of samples to find exceptional lots worthy of small-batch roasting.

02

Profile

Each bean gets a custom roast profile developed through dozens of test roasts to unlock its best flavors.

03

Roast

Small-batch roasting on our Loring S15 Falcon. Every roast is logged, analyzed, and quality-checked.

04

Brew

Expert baristas extract each coffee's potential using precision brewing equipment calibrated daily.
