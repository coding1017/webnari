FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know about working with PristineClean.

What types of facilities do you clean?
Commercial offices, medical facilities, warehouses, retail, restaurants, schools, gyms, and residential properties. If it has a floor and walls, we can make it pristine.
Are you licensed and insured?
Yes. License #CLN2024-0088, $2M general liability, and all team members are background-checked and bonded.
Do you use eco-friendly products?
Green Seal and EPA Safer Choice certified products as standard. Effective against bacteria and viruses while safe for people, pets, and the environment.
How quickly can you start?
Standard services within 24-48 hours. Emergency cleanup available 24/7 with same-day dispatch across Miami-Dade County.
What does the satisfaction guarantee cover?
Contact us within 24 hours and we re-clean affected areas at no charge. No questions asked, no fine print.
Do you offer recurring discounts?
Weekly clients save up to 25%, bi-weekly 15%, monthly 10%. Contact us for a custom quote based on your facility size.
What are your operating hours?
Monday-Saturday, 7 AM - 7 PM for regular scheduling. After-hours and weekend cleaning available for commercial clients. Emergency services run 24/7.
