Questions

Frequently Asked

What documents should I bring to our first meeting?

+
Please bring prior year tax returns, recent bank statements, invoices, expense receipts, payroll records, and any relevant business documents. We'll provide a detailed checklist when we schedule your consultation.

How is your pricing structured?

+
We offer fixed-fee pricing for most services based on the complexity and scope of your needs. This means you know the cost upfront with no surprise hourly charges. We'll provide a detailed quote after our discovery call.

How long does tax preparation typically take?

+
For most clients with organized records, we can prepare and deliver your completed tax return within one to two weeks. Complex situations may require additional time. We always communicate timelines upfront.

Do you work with startups?

+
Absolutely. We specialize in helping new business owners with entity formation, bookkeeping setup, payroll implementation, and strategic planning during those critical early years.

Can you help with business entity selection?

+
Yes. We provide guidance on whether a sole proprietorship, LLC, S-corp, or C-corp structure is best for your situation based on tax efficiency, liability protection, and growth plans.

Do you offer virtual or remote services?

+
Yes. While we're located in Brickell, we serve clients throughout South Florida and beyond. We conduct meetings, file sharing, and consultations securely online for your convenience.
