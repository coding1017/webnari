Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the booking? ▼

All packages include DJ services, sound system, professional mixing, and MC services. Premium packages add full lighting rigs and additional support staff. Equipment setup and breakdown are included in all bookings.

Can you take music requests? ▼

Absolutely! We love working with clients to customize their music selection. Just let us know your preferences during the consultation, and we'll build a playlist tailored to your event and crowd.

Do you have backup equipment? ▼

Yes, we carry backup equipment for all critical systems including turntables, mixers, and speakers. Your event's success is our priority, and we're prepared for any situation.

What's the travel fee for events outside Miami? ▼

We serve all of South Florida with no additional travel fees within our service areas. For events beyond our standard coverage, we'll discuss travel costs during your consultation.

What's your cancellation policy? ▼

Cancellations made 30+ days before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations within 30 days may forfeit the deposit. We recommend event insurance and will work with you on rescheduling if possible.

Do you offer multiple DJs for large events? ▼

Yes! Our Ultimate package includes two DJs plus a full technical team. For larger festivals or multi-day events, we can arrange additional DJs and crew. Contact us for custom quotes.
