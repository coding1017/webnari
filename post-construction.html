Post-Construction Cleanup

From new builds to major renovations, our multi-phase cleaning process removes every trace of construction so your space is move-in ready from day one.

Book This Service Call (305) 555-0400
Service Features

Everything your build site needs.

Our certified crews tackle every post-construction challenge with industrial-grade equipment and proven multi-phase processes.

Rough Clean Debris Removal
Bulk removal of construction waste including drywall scraps, wood cutoffs, packaging materials, and leftover building supplies.
Fine Dust Removal
HEPA-filtered vacuuming and damp-wiping of every surface to eliminate drywall dust, sawdust, and airborne particulates throughout the space.
Window & Glass Cleaning
Removal of paint splatters, sticker residue, and construction film from all windows, glass doors, mirrors, and glass partitions.
Floor Finishing & Polishing
Machine scrubbing, buffing, and polishing for tile, hardwood, concrete, and VCT flooring to restore original finish and shine.
HVAC Vent Cleaning
Deep cleaning of all air vents, registers, and accessible ductwork to remove construction dust before the HVAC system is activated.
Paint Overspray Removal
Careful removal of paint drips, overspray, and splatters from floors, fixtures, countertops, and hardware using safe solvents.
Fixture Polishing
Detailed polishing of all installed fixtures including faucets, handles, light switches, outlet covers, and cabinet hardware.
Final Detail Clean
White-glove inspection and touch-up cleaning of every corner, ledge, closet, and cabinet interior to ensure move-in readiness.
What's Included

Four pillars of clean.

Every post-construction project follows our structured approach to deliver consistent, flawless results.

01
Debris Removal
Complete removal of construction waste, scrap materials, packing, and bulk debris. We handle hauling and proper disposal so your site is cleared fast.
02
Dust Elimination
HEPA-filtered equipment removes fine particulates from every surface, vent, ledge, and hidden crevice. Safe for occupied and pre-occupancy environments.
03
Surface Finishing
Professional floor scrubbing, window detailing, fixture polishing, and paint overspray removal to restore every surface to its intended finish.
04
Final Inspection
White-glove walkthrough with a detailed checklist covering every room, closet, and fixture. We do not leave until the space passes our standards.
Our Process

Three phases to pristine.

Each phase builds on the last, progressively transforming your construction site into a polished, move-in ready space.

1
Phase 1: Rough Clean
Bulk debris removal, sweeping, and initial vacuuming. We clear the heavy construction waste so the space is safe and accessible for detail work.
2
Phase 2: Detail Clean
HEPA vacuuming, damp-wiping all surfaces, window cleaning, paint overspray removal, and HVAC vent cleaning throughout the entire space.
3
Phase 3: Final Touch
Floor polishing, fixture detailing, white-glove inspection, and touch-ups. The space is handed over in move-in condition with our satisfaction guarantee.
FAQ

Questions about post-construction cleaning.

How long does a post-construction cleanup take?
Timelines depend on the project size and condition. A standard residential unit typically takes 1-2 days, while larger commercial spaces may require 3-5 days. We provide a detailed timeline estimate during our initial walkthrough.
Do you handle all three phases or just the final clean?
We offer all three phases as a complete package or individually based on your needs. Many general contractors bring us in for the rough clean immediately after construction wraps, then schedule detail and final touch phases as finishing trades complete their work.
Can you work around ongoing finishing trades?
Absolutely. We coordinate with painters, flooring installers, and other finishing trades daily. Our phased approach is designed to work alongside ongoing construction activities without disrupting your project timeline.
Are your crews insured for construction site work?
Yes. We carry $2M in general liability coverage and all team members are background-checked, OSHA-trained, and bonded. We can provide certificates of insurance for your project requirements. License #CLN2024-0088.
