Know the Risk

Is Your Panel a Hazard?

Many South Florida homes still have Federal Pacific Stab-Lok panels, Zinsco panels, or old fuse boxes — all of which are known fire hazards. These panels can fail to trip during overloads, allowing wires to overheat silently inside your walls. If your home was built before 1990, there's a real chance your electrical panel is putting your family at risk every day.

We inspect every panel at no charge and give you a straight answer — no upselling, no scare tactics. Just facts and flat-rate pricing.

Warning Signs
⚠️

Breakers that trip frequently or won't reset

🔥

Burning smell from the panel or nearby outlets

💡

Lights that flicker or dim unexpectedly

🌡️

Panel feels warm to the touch

🔧

You have a fuse box instead of breakers

🚗
