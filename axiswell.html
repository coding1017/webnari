Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance? −
Yes! We accept most major insurance plans including Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Humana, and many others. We also accept Medicare and offer affordable cash plans for uninsured patients.
What should I bring to my first appointment? +
Please bring your insurance card, government-issued ID, and any relevant medical records or imaging results. Arrive 10-15 minutes early to complete our intake form.
Is chiropractic care safe? +
Absolutely. Chiropractic care is one of the safest forms of healthcare when performed by a licensed professional. Our doctors are fully credentialed and use evidence-based techniques.
How often should I receive treatment? +
Treatment frequency depends on your condition. Acute problems may require 2-3 visits per week initially, while chronic conditions might need 1-2 visits weekly. We'll customize a plan for you.
Will I hear popping or cracking sounds? +
Some adjustments produce a sound called joint cavitation, which is air bubbles releasing from the joint. It's completely normal and not dangerous. We also offer gentle techniques without popping if you prefer.
Can children benefit from chiropractic care? +
Yes! Children can benefit greatly from chiropractic care. We use specialized techniques designed specifically for young patients to support healthy development and address growth-related issues.
