Our Philosophy

Movement is medicine.

"Physical fitness is the first requisite of happiness." — Joseph Pilates

CoreForm was founded in 2019 on a single conviction: the body responds to precision, not punishment. We reject the noise of fitness culture and embrace the quiet discipline of intentional movement. Every session at CoreForm is guided by the six foundational Pilates principles — Centering, Concentration, Control, Precision, Breath, and Flow. Our instructors hold advanced certifications and bring clinical knowledge to every class, ensuring you move well, not just often.

Grip socks are required for Reformer classes (available for purchase at the studio, $14). Wear form-fitting, comfortable activewear — nothing too loose. We recommend avoiding heavy perfumes out of respect for other clients.
We ask that new clients arrive 10 minutes early to complete an intake form and receive a brief studio orientation. Returning clients should aim to arrive at least 5 minutes before class begins.
