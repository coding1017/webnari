INTEL BRIEFING
What training methods do you use? ∨
We use science-based positive reinforcement methods exclusively. No dominance-based techniques, no punishment, only reward-driven conditioning. Our approach is modern, humane, and proven effective by behavioral science and thousands of successful cases.
How long does training typically take? ∨
Basic obedience programs range from 4-6 weeks depending on your dog's starting point. Board & Train is 2 weeks minimum. Behavior modification varies based on the severity of issues, typically 6-8 weeks. We'll assess your dog and give you a realistic timeline at your consultation.
Do you work with all dog breeds and ages? ∨
Yes! We successfully train everything from 8-week-old puppies to senior dogs. All breeds are welcome, including those with behavioral issues. Our certified trainers have experience with hundreds of breed types and temperament variations.
What's your success guarantee? ∨
We maintain a 98% permanent behavior improvement rate. All graduates receive lifetime support including free follow-up sessions, refresher training, and 24/7 consultation access. If your dog regresses after training, we'll work with you at no additional cost.
Can you handle aggression and serious behavior problems? ∨
Yes, behavior modification is our specialty. We successfully work with aggression, anxiety, fear reactivity, resource guarding, and complex behavioral issues. Our specialized program addresses root causes with science-backed protocols and custom conditioning plans.
Do you offer Board & Train programs? ∨
Absolutely. Our Board & Train programs provide intensive 24/7 training in a controlled environment. Your dog stays with us for 2+ weeks, receiving daily conditioning from our senior trainers. Ideal for busy owners or dogs needing immersive behavior work.
