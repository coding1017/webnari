The Story

Born in the woods.
Built for Miami.

Timber & Iron was built by lifelong outdoorsmen who couldn't find a proper axe throwing venue in South Florida — so they built one. Our 6,000 sq ft facility in Doral houses 12 professional-grade throwing lanes, a full bar, and the most knowledgeable staff this side of the Everglades.

We're World Axe Throwing League (WATL) certified, meaning every lane, target, and safety protocol meets international competition standards. Whether you're throwing for the first time or chasing a podium spot, Timber & Iron is built for you.

Safety Rules
01
Closed-toe shoes required at all times in the throwing area. No sandals, heels, or bare feet.
02
Minimum age 12 years old. Ages 12–17 require a waiver signed by a parent or legal guardian present on-site.
03
Do not approach the target board while others are throwing. Wait for the all-clear from your lane coach.
04
Alcohol is permitted but management reserves the right to remove any participant who appears intoxicated.
05
All axes remain in the throwing lane. Never carry an axe outside your designated throwing area.

Questions?

Good aim means knowing the rules.

Everything you need to know before your first throw at Timber & Iron.

Walk-ins are always welcome during open hours — just show up. We recommend booking in advance for groups of 5 or more, or for private coached sessions and party lanes, to guarantee your lane. Use our online form or call (786) 555-0960.
Absolutely. Every session begins with a thorough safety briefing from one of our certified coaches. Axe throwing is statistically safer than many common activities when proper safety protocols are followed — and we don't cut corners.
Closed-toe shoes are required — no sandals, heels, or bare feet. Comfortable clothing you can move in is ideal. We keep axes away from your body, so there's no special protective gear needed.
Yes! Outside food is welcome (we love a good birthday cake in the venue). We have a full bar with craft beers, cocktails, and non-alcoholic options. For large groups we can coordinate catering with partner restaurants.
Minimum throwing age is 12 years old. Ages 12–17 require a parent or legal guardian to be present and sign a waiver on their behalf. Big Blade lanes are 18+ only.
Yes — and it's one of our specialties. We can host groups from 10 to 120+ with full venue buyout, custom tournament brackets, catering coordination, branded materials, and invoicing for business expense purposes. Contact us for a custom quote.
