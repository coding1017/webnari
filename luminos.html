Our Philosophy

Enhance, never
alter.

At Luminos, we believe the highest form of beauty is authenticity. Our approach begins with listening — understanding your goals, your lifestyle, your unique anatomy — and building a treatment plan that reveals the best version of you. No cookie-cutter protocols. No frozen faces. Just artful science.

Medical-grade safety first. Every treatment is supervised by a board-certified physician, with full medical histories reviewed prior to any procedure.

Individualized treatment planning. We reject one-size-fits-all protocols. Your facial anatomy, skin type, and aesthetic goals guide every decision.

Natural-looking results, always. Our signature technique prioritizes symmetry, proportion, and subtlety — people should notice you look wonderful, not that you had work done.

Botox and Dysport results typically last 3–4 months for first-time patients and 4–6 months for long-term patients as muscles become trained to remain relaxed. We recommend maintenance appointments every 3–4 months to keep results consistent and optimal.

When performed by qualified medical professionals, dermal fillers are extremely safe. At Luminos, we exclusively use FDA-approved hyaluronic acid fillers — Juvederm, Restylane, and Sculptra — which are fully reversible with hyaluronidase if needed. All injections are performed by board-certified MDs or NPs in a medical setting with full emergency protocols on-hand.

Most neuromodulator and filler treatments have minimal to no downtime — you can return to normal activities immediately, though we recommend avoiding intense exercise and alcohol for 24 hours. Laser treatments and chemical peels may involve 2–7 days of redness or peeling depending on the intensity. We will give you a full pre and post-care protocol at your consultation.

Your complimentary consultation is 45 minutes with your practitioner — no rushing, no pressure. We'll review your complete medical history, discuss your aesthetic goals, perform a facial assessment, and present a written, prioritized treatment plan. You're never obligated to book a treatment that day. We want you to feel fully informed and comfortable before any decision.

Our patients range from their mid-20s to their 70s. Many younger patients start with preventative Botox in their late 20s or early 30s to slow the formation of dynamic lines. Others begin with laser treatments for sun damage or acne scarring. There's no "right age" — what matters is your goals and the appropriate treatment for your skin at this stage of life.
