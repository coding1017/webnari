FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need a partner?
Nope. All group classes include partner rotation, so you'll dance with multiple people. Many of our students come solo and it's completely normal.
What should I wear?
Comfortable clothes that let you move freely. Smooth-soled shoes work best — avoid sneakers with heavy tread. We sell dance shoes at the studio if you need them.
I have no rhythm. Can I still learn?
Absolutely. Rhythm is learned, not innate. Our beginner classes start with basic timing and body isolation before any fancy moves. You'd be surprised how quickly it clicks.
Do you offer drop-in classes?
Yes. Every group class is available as a drop-in for $20. We also offer 10-class packs ($160), monthly unlimited ($99/mo), and annual memberships ($79/mo).
Can you choreograph our wedding first dance?
It's one of our specialties. We'll listen to your song, learn your style, and create a custom routine that looks amazing and feels natural. Packages start at $450 for 6 sessions.
Where are you located?
We're in the heart of Little Havana at 1580 SW 8th Street, Miami FL 33135. Street parking available and we're a 5-minute walk from the 8th Street Metromover station.
