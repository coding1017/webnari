The Science

HOW THE MATH WORKS

1
Calculate BMR
We use the Mifflin-St Jeor equation to find your Basal Metabolic Rate -- the calories your body burns at complete rest.
2
Apply Activity
Your BMR is multiplied by an activity factor (1.2 to 1.9) to get your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).
3
Adjust for Goal
We subtract 400 cal for fat loss or add 350 cal for muscle gain to find your daily calorie target.
4
Split Macros
Calories are divided into optimal ratios of protein, carbs, and fats based on your specific goal.
