What's Hiding in Your Air

The Hidden Cost of
Poor Indoor Air Quality

The EPA ranks indoor air pollution among the top five environmental health risks. South Florida's heat and humidity make local homes especially vulnerable to these four core problems.

Allergens & Particulates

Pollen, pet dander, dust mites, and fine particulate matter (PM2.5) circulate through your ductwork every time your system runs. Standard 1-inch filters capture less than 30% of these particles, leaving the rest to trigger allergies, asthma, and respiratory irritation.

Excess Humidity

Miami-Dade humidity routinely exceeds 80% indoors without proper dehumidification. High moisture levels promote mold growth, make your home feel hotter, cause wood warping, and put extra strain on your AC system — raising energy bills and shortening equipment life.

VOCs & Chemical Pollutants

Volatile organic compounds off-gas from paint, cleaning products, furniture, and building materials. In a sealed Florida home, VOC concentrations can be 2 to 5 times higher than outdoor levels — linked to headaches, dizziness, and long-term respiratory damage.

Mold & Microbial Growth

Florida's climate is ideal for mold colonization in ductwork, evaporator coils, and air handlers. Mold spores spread through every room the moment your fan turns on — causing musty odors, property damage, and serious health consequences for occupants.

Our Air Quality Systems

Five Solutions for
Dramatically Cleaner Air

Every home is different. Our certified technicians assess your space and recommend a layered strategy — because clean air is the result of multiple systems working together.

01

HEPA Whole-Home Filtration

True HEPA media filters integrate directly into your air handler and capture 99.97% of particles 0.3 microns or larger — including pollen, pet dander, dust mites, and most airborne bacteria. We size and install the correct filter box for your system's airflow requirements to avoid pressure-drop issues.

02

UV Air Purifiers

Germicidal UV-C lights mounted inside your air handler neutralize mold spores, bacteria, and viruses on contact — including those too small for any filter. AirPro installs dual-lamp systems that treat both the coil surface and the airstream 24 hours a day, eliminating bio-growth at its source.

03

Whole-Home Dehumidifiers

A dedicated whole-home dehumidifier works independently of your AC to pull moisture out of the air and maintain relative humidity between 45–55% year-round. Unlike portable units, it handles the entire home and drains automatically — no buckets, no guesswork, no wet-feeling rooms.

04

Professional Duct Cleaning

Years of dust, debris, pet hair, and microbial growth accumulate inside your ductwork. Our negative-pressure duct cleaning service vacuums and brushes your entire duct system, then applies an EPA-registered antimicrobial treatment — removing the reservoir of contaminants that standard filters never see.

05

Smart Thermostats & IAQ Monitors

See your air quality in real time. We install smart thermostats and connected indoor air quality monitors that track PM2.5, CO2, humidity, and VOC levels — then automatically adjust your HVAC system to keep readings in healthy ranges. Know exactly what you're breathing, every hour of the day.
