About Petal & Thorn

Lucia's Vision

Since 2018, Petal & Thorn has been Coconut Grove's destination for artisan floristry. Founded by Lucia, a lifelong lover of flowers and botanical beauty, our studio celebrates the intersection of nature and careful craft.

Every arrangement tells a story. We source flowers locally whenever possible, honor the seasons, and believe that flowers should be as fresh and vibrant as the moments they celebrate.

Every stem tells a story.

Whether you're celebrating a milestone, expressing condolences, or simply bringing joy to someone's day, we're here to create something beautiful for you.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far do you deliver?

+

We deliver throughout Coconut Grove, Coral Gables, Brickell, South Miami, Key Biscayne, Pinecrest, Miami Beach, Wynwood, Downtown Miami, and Little Havana. For deliveries outside these areas, contact us directly.

Can I order a custom arrangement?

+

Absolutely! We love custom orders. Simply fill out our contact form or call us at (305) 555-1450 to discuss your vision. We work with any color palette, flower preference, or theme you have in mind.

Do you provide arrangements for weddings and events?

+

Yes! We specialize in wedding florals and event arrangements. We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance for weddings. Contact us to schedule a consultation with Lucia.

What are your seasonal offerings?

+

We celebrate each season with special arrangements featuring seasonal blooms. Check with us monthly for limited-edition offerings. Seasonality means fresher, more beautiful flowers.

How do I care for my arrangement?

+

Every arrangement comes with care instructions. Generally: trim stems at a 45-degree angle, change water every 2–3 days, remove dead petals, and keep flowers away from direct sunlight and heat.

Do you offer subscription services?

+

Yes! Weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly flower subscriptions are available. We'll deliver a fresh seasonal arrangement on your chosen schedule. Contact us for pricing and details.
