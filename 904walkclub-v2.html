Our Mission
Building Community,
One Step at a Time

904 Walk Club is Jacksonville's community walking club — dedicated to building health, connection, and motivation through the simple act of walking together. Every Wednesday, we host free group walks at local landmarks.

All fitness levels welcome. No pace requirements. No fees. Just show up, walk, and connect.
