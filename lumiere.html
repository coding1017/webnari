About
Lumière & Co.

Founded in Miami in 2017, Lumière & Co. was born from one simple belief: that your wedding photographs should feel as alive in 50 years as they do today.

We approach every wedding as documentary journalists who also happen to be visual artists. We are always present, always aware, always invisible — until the moment calls for direction. The result is a body of work that is both honest and beautiful.

Our studio is based in Coral Gables, and we photograph weddings across all of South Florida and beyond.

01
Inquiry & Availability Check

Fill out our inquiry form with your date and venue. We'll confirm availability and set up a complimentary 30-minute call to learn about your vision and personalities. No pressure, always genuine.

A signed contract and 30% retainer secures your date. Your engagement session happens 3–12 months before the wedding — this is where we learn to work together and build the trust that shows in your wedding images.

We arrive early, stay late, and capture everything in between. We work quietly, efficiently, and with joy. Our goal is to be your least stressful vendor — and our couples always tell us we are.

Your private online gallery is delivered within 3–5 weeks. Every image is individually curated and edited to our signature warm, film-inspired style. You'll receive a preview teaser within 48 hours of your wedding.

Common Questions

Before
You Inquire

We want you to feel completely informed before reaching out. Here are the questions we hear most from our couples.

Still Have Questions? Ask Us
How far in advance should we book?
+
We recommend reaching out at least 12–18 months before your wedding, especially for spring and fall dates which book earliest. However, we do occasionally have late availability — it's always worth inquiring. We hold dates only with a signed contract and retainer.
Do you travel for destination weddings?
+
Absolutely — travel is one of our favorite parts of this work. We've photographed weddings in Mexico, Europe, the Caribbean, and throughout the US. Travel costs (flights, accommodation, per diem) are covered by the client and quoted transparently when you inquire. No hidden fees.
What is your editing style?
+
Our editing style is warm, film-inspired, and cinematic. We keep skin tones natural and flattering, with rich shadows, soft highlights, and a timeless quality that avoids the trendy filters that can date a photograph quickly. You can view full galleries (not just highlights) by request during our inquiry process.
How many images will we receive?
+
Our galleries typically deliver 50–80 fully edited images per hour of coverage. For a 10-hour wedding with two photographers, you can expect 600–900 images. We don't promise a specific count — we promise every image in your gallery will be one we'd hang on our own walls.
Can we see full wedding galleries before booking?
+
Yes — we believe this is essential. Highlight galleries are curated for beauty; complete wedding galleries show you how we work through an entire day across all lighting conditions, candid moments, and family formals. We provide full gallery access upon request during your inquiry consultation.
