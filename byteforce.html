FAQ

Common questions before you bring us your device.

Q01: How much does computer repair cost?
›
Most common repairs range from $50–$200. Screen replacements are typically $80–$180 depending on the model. Virus removal is $75–$120. Data recovery starts at $100 for logical failures. We always give you a quote before starting work — no surprises.
Q02: Do you work on both Macs and PCs?
›
Yes — we service all major platforms: Windows (all versions), macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, and Chrome OS. Laptops, desktops, tablets, and smartphones. All brands welcome: Apple, Dell, HP, Lenovo, Asus, Acer, Samsung, and more.
Q03: Can you recover data from a water-damaged device?
›
Often yes, depending on how quickly you bring it in and the extent of damage. Turn the device off immediately and do not try to charge it. The sooner you bring it to us, the higher the chance of successful recovery. We'll diagnose it for free and let you know the odds before charging you anything.
Q04: Do you offer remote support?
›
Yes. Our remote support service uses secure, industry-standard screen-sharing tools. We can fix most software issues, malware, email configuration, software installation, and slow performance remotely — without you leaving home or the office. Available 24/7 for plan subscribers.
Q05: What is your warranty on repairs?
›
All ByteForce repairs come with a 90-day workmanship warranty. If the same issue returns within 90 days due to our work, we'll fix it at no additional charge. Parts warranties vary by manufacturer — typically 90 days to 1 year depending on the component.
