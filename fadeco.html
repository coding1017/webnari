Why Choose Us

Fade & Co. isn't just a haircut. It's a ritual. From the moment you sit in the chair to the moment you leave looking sharp, every detail matters to us.

—

Licensed Master Barbers

Every barber at Fade & Co. holds a Florida state barbering license and has minimum 5 years of professional experience. We hire for skill, passion, and professionalism — nothing less.

Premium Products Only

We use professional-grade shears, clippers, straight razors, and grooming products. From the hot towel to the final product application, only the best touches your hair and skin.

How It Works

Getting fresh has never been easier.

Four simple steps from first call to fresh cut.

Book or Walk In

Book your appointment online or just walk through the door. We welcome both — and we'll always try to get you in the same day.

Consultation

Your barber talks you through the cut before touching your hair — discussing your style, face shape, and what works best for you specifically.

Precision Cut

Your barber executes the cut with care and precision — no rushing, no shortcuts. Every line crisp, every blend seamless, every detail locked in.

Finish & Style

Hot towel neck clean, edge lineup touchup, product application, and a final mirror review — you leave looking sharper than when you walked in.
