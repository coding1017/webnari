FAQ

Plan questions.

Everything you need to know about our recurring maintenance plans.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?
No. All maintenance plans are month-to-month. We earn your business every visit. You can pause or cancel with 7 days written notice.
Can I change my cleaning frequency?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Your dedicated team stays the same regardless of frequency.
What happens if I need to skip a visit?
Let us know at least 48 hours in advance and we will reschedule at no charge. Last-minute cancellations may be subject to a trip fee. Holiday weeks can be skipped with advance notice.
How is the custom quote determined?
We factor in your facility size (square footage), number of restrooms, type of flooring, any specialty areas, and your chosen frequency. We provide a detailed line-item quote after a free walkthrough.
