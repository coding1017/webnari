Frequently Asked

How long does a typical project take? +

Most projects take 3-5 business days for installation, depending on complexity. The entire process from consultation to completion typically spans 4-6 weeks, including design and fabrication.

Do you offer a warranty? +

Yes. All Neatform systems come with a lifetime warranty on craftsmanship and materials. We stand behind our work completely.

What areas do you serve? +

We service all of South Florida, including Miami, Coral Gables, Brickell, Doral, Kendall, and surrounding communities. We offer free in-home consultations throughout the region.

Can I customize the design? +

Absolutely. Every Neatform system is custom-designed for your specific space, lifestyle, and aesthetic preferences. We work with you every step of the way.

What materials do you use? +

We use premium FSC-certified wood, soft-close hardware, durable finishes, and quality organizational components. All materials are sourced from trusted suppliers and built to last decades.

Do you handle the removal and disposal? +

Yes. Our white-glove service includes removing old fixtures, disposing of materials responsibly, and leaving your space completely clean and ready to enjoy.
