Industries We Serve

HVAC Solutions for
Every Business Type

Commercial HVAC is not one-size-fits-all. Different business types have radically different load profiles, operating hours, and code requirements. We have the experience to design and maintain the right system for your specific environment.

Retail Stores

Customer comfort directly affects dwell time and sales. We design zoned systems for retail spaces that maintain consistent temperatures at peak foot traffic hours — even when doors open constantly in South Florida's heat.

Restaurants & Food Service

Kitchen exhaust, makeup air, and dining zone comfort must be balanced precisely. Improperly designed restaurant HVAC leads to hot kitchens, draft complaints, and health code violations. We spec and install systems that meet all commercial kitchen ventilation codes.

Office Buildings

Multi-tenant office buildings need flexible zone control, after-hours override capability, and energy management that keeps costs low without sacrificing tenant comfort. We integrate BAS-compatible systems and smart scheduling for maximum efficiency.

Medical & Dental Offices

Healthcare facilities require HEPA filtration, positive and negative pressure rooms, precise humidity control, and 24/7 reliability. We hold certifications for medical-grade HVAC installation and serve dozens of clinics across South Florida.

Warehouses & Distribution

Large open spaces, high ceilings, and industrial equipment create extreme heat loads. We deploy industrial makeup air units, destratification fans, and evaporative pre-cooling strategies to keep warehouse environments safe and productive for workers.

Hotels & Hospitality

Guest satisfaction scores are directly tied to room temperature control. We install and maintain PTAC units, chilled water systems, and lobby cooling systems for hotels of all sizes — with priority service agreements to ensure zero guest complaints.

What We Install and Service

Commercial HVAC
Services

Our commercial team is factory-trained on major equipment brands and holds all required Florida commercial contractor licenses.

01

Rooftop Package Units (RTUs)

RTUs are the workhorse of commercial HVAC — cost-effective, easy to service, and suitable for most single-story commercial buildings. We handle complete RTU replacement, installation of new units on new construction, and mechanical curb adaptation for retrofits. All brands serviced: Carrier, Trane, Lennox, Daikin, York.

02

Variable Refrigerant Flow (VRF) Systems

VRF and VRV systems offer unmatched zoning flexibility for multi-use commercial spaces. A single outdoor unit can serve dozens of individually controlled indoor units — delivering simultaneous heating and cooling in different zones while using significantly less energy than conventional systems.

03

Chiller Systems

For buildings over 50,000 square feet, central chiller plants offer the most efficient large-scale cooling solution available. AirPro engineers and installs air-cooled and water-cooled chiller systems, cooling towers, and chilled water distribution piping — complete turnkey from engineering to commissioning.

04

Preventive Maintenance Contracts

Commercial HVAC failures always happen at the worst possible time — a busy Saturday, a heat wave, a full hotel. Our PM contracts put AirPro technicians on a fixed schedule for inspections, coil cleaning, belt checks, refrigerant verification, and system performance logging — stopping failures before they start.
