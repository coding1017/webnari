Frequently Asked Questions

What documents can you notarize?
+
We notarize most legal documents including real estate deeds, mortgages, power of attorney, wills, affidavits, loan documents, and more. Some documents have restrictions—contact us to verify.
How quickly can you come?
+
We typically schedule appointments within 24 hours. For urgent matters, same-day service is often available. Call us to check availability for your preferred time.
Do you charge travel fees?
+
Travel fees apply for appointments outside our primary service area or after hours. Contact us for a quote for your specific location and timing.
What identification do I need?
+
Florida law requires valid government-issued ID with a photo and signature. Acceptable forms include driver's license, passport, and state ID. Expired IDs are generally not accepted.
Are you NNA certified?
+
Yes, Maria is an NNA (National Notary Association) Certified Signing Agent with specialized training in loan closings and complex transaction documents.
Can you handle international documents?
+
Yes. We provide Florida Apostille certification for documents needed internationally. Processing typically takes 3-5 business days.
