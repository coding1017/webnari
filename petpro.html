Our Process

How a Visit Works

From booking to pick-up, here's what to expect when you bring your furry friend to Paws & Co.

1

Book Online or Call

Choose your service, pick a date and time that works, and tell us about your pet's breed, size, and any special needs.

2

Meet & Greet

We greet your pet calmly, do a quick coat assessment, and discuss any specific requests or concerns before we begin.

3

Grooming Session

Your pet gets pampered one-on-one. We work at their pace, take breaks when needed, and use treats and praise throughout.

4

Fresh & Fabulous

We send you a photo, apply a finishing spritz, tie on a bandana, and your pet is ready for pick-up looking amazing.
