Chef Adriana's Story

With 18 years of culinary experience and a deep passion for bringing people together, Chef Adriana has crafted Ember & Oak as a celebration of locally sourced ingredients and time-honored techniques. Every dish is prepared from scratch, every event is treated as a masterpiece.

Growing up in Coconut Grove, Adriana learned that the best meals are more than food—they're memories, connections, and stories shared around a table. This philosophy guides every catering event we undertake.

From farm to table, from consultation to dessert, Ember & Oak brings warmth, creativity, and genuine care to your most precious moments.

Common Questions

What is your minimum guest count? +

We accommodate intimate gatherings of 15 guests and up to large celebrations of 300+. For smaller events, please contact us directly to discuss options.

How do you handle dietary restrictions and allergies? +

We take all dietary needs seriously. During consultation, we discuss allergies, vegetarian/vegan preferences, religious requirements, and health concerns. We prepare separate items when necessary and ensure clear labeling and handling protocols.

Can we do a tasting before booking? +

Absolutely! We strongly encourage tastings. This allows you to experience our work firsthand and collaborate on menu refinement. Tastings typically take place at your venue or our location by appointment.

What is your booking and payment timeline? +

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance for best availability. A deposit of 50% secures your date, with the balance due two weeks before your event. Flexible payment plans available upon request.

Do you provide service staff, bartenders, and rentals? +

Yes! We provide full-service staffing including servers, bartenders, and coordinators. We can also recommend trusted rental partners for linens, china, glassware, and furniture.

Do you cater events outside of Coconut Grove? +

We regularly serve all of South Florida and are available for destination events throughout Florida. We can arrange travel, on-site setup, and full coordination. Speak with us about your location.
