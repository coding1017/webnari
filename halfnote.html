About HalfNote

HalfNote Studios was founded by a collective of professional musicians who believe that music education should be accessible, inspiring, and tailored to each student's unique goals.

We've spent years teaching in Coconut Grove and across South Florida, and we're passionate about helping you discover your musical voice—whether you're picking up an instrument for the first time or honing a lifetime of practice.

Our instructors are accomplished performers and patient educators. We teach everything from classical foundations to contemporary songwriting, always with encouragement and flexibility.

Frequently Asked

Do you offer trial lessons?

Yes! We offer a free 15-minute consultation and a reduced-rate first lesson ($20) so you can get a feel for our teaching style and the studio. We're confident you'll love it.

Can I rent or borrow an instrument?

We partner with local music rental shops and can recommend affordable options for piano, guitar, drums, violin, and more. We'll help you find something suitable for your budget and goals.

How often do you hold recitals?

We hold student recitals quarterly (roughly every 3 months) at our studio in Coconut Grove. Participation is optional but highly encouraged—it's a wonderful way to celebrate progress and perform in a supportive environment.

What's the difference between group classes and private lessons?

Private lessons (one-on-one) offer personalized instruction tailored to your pace and goals. Group classes are more social and affordable, great for foundational learning and ensemble skills. Many students do both.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

We ask for 24 hours notice for cancellations. If you cancel with 24+ hours notice, you can reschedule free of charge. Same-day cancellations are charged as a lesson. Life happens—we're flexible within reason.

What should I bring to my first lesson?

Just yourself and enthusiasm! We have instruments available, or bring your own if you have one. Your instructor will guide you on music materials and any books needed after your first session.
